Hateruma Island波照間島

Japan's southernmost inhabited island — home to the dazzling Nishihama Beach and some of the country's darkest, clearest night skies. Beautiful, remote, and reached by one of Yaeyama's roughest, most-cancelled ferries.日本最南端の有人島。まばゆいニシ浜と、国内でも屈指の暗く澄んだ星空で知られます。美しく遠く、そして八重山でも屈指に荒れて欠航の多いフェリーで渡る島です。

Hateruma lies out beyond Iriomote in the open ocean — the last inhabited land before the sea runs on to the tropics. It's a small, flat island of sugarcane and coral-stone walls, and people come for three things: the impossibly blue Nishihama Beach, the sense of standing at the southern edge of Japan, and night skies dark enough that the Southern Cross rises into view in season. 波照間は西表の先、外洋に浮かぶ島。ここから先は海が南の熱帯へと続く、日本最南端の有人地です。サトウキビとサンゴの石垣の小さな平坦な島で、旅の目的は3つ。ありえないほど青いニシ浜、日本の南の果てに立つ感覚、そして季節には南十字星が昇るほど暗い星空です。

What to see見どころ

Nishihama Beach is the island's one swimming beach and regularly rated among Japan's most beautiful — brilliant white sand and blue water. The Southernmost Point of Japan monument stands on the Takanasaki cliffs on the southeast coast. After dark the island is one of Japan's best star-gazing spots, with the Southern Cross visible roughly late April to mid-June. (The public star-viewing tower has been closed for ageing facilities — check its status — but the open skies are superb anywhere away from lights.) ニシ浜は島唯一の遊泳ビーチで、日本有数の美しさと評されます。真っ白な砂と青い海。南東の高那崎の崖には日本最南端の碑。夜は国内屈指の星空スポットで、南十字星はおおむね4月下旬〜6月中旬に見えます。（星空観測タワーは老朽化で閉鎖中との情報あり。状況は要確認ですが、灯りのない場所ならどこでも空は見事です。）

Getting there & the ferry — read this first行き方とフェリー（まず確認）

Only Anei Kanko runs the route from Ishigaki, about 60–70 minutes on a high-speed boat (longer on the larger vessel). Past Iriomote it crosses fully open ocean, so it is one of the roughest and most frequently cancelled routes in all of Yaeyama — in the winter north-wind months cancellation can exceed half the days, and the crossing itself is famously bumpy. It can strand you either on Ishigaki or on Hateruma. 石垣島からは安栄観光のみが運航、高速船で約60〜70分（大型船はさらに長い）。西表を過ぎると完全な外洋を横切るため、八重山全体でも屈指に荒れて欠航が多い路線。冬の北風の時期は欠航が半分を超える月もあり、揺れの激しさでも有名です。石垣・波照間どちらでも足止めになり得ます。

Plan for cancellations欠航を前提に計画を Build in buffer days and never schedule this route tight against your flight home. Bring motion-sickness medication, carry cash (limited shops/ATM), and book the small pool of lodging well ahead. The island is easily circled by rental bike, scooter or car. Check our morning forecast — and the operator's day-of status page — before every leg. 予備日を必ず確保し、帰りの飛行機とギリギリの日程を組まないこと。酔い止めを持参し、現金も用意を（商店・ATMは少ない）。少ない宿は早めに予約を。島はレンタサイクル・スクーター・レンタカーで一周できます。往復とも、朝の予報と運航会社の当日運航状況を確認しましょう。
Where to stay

Stay on Hateruma波照間に泊まる

An overnight is worth it for the star skies — small family-run minshuku and a backpacker hostel. But the ferry is often cancelled, so book flexibly and keep a buffer day. Reserve well ahead (small inventory).星空のために宿泊する価値あり。小さな家族経営の民宿とバックパッカー宿があります。ただしフェリーは欠航が多いので、柔軟に予約し予備日を。宿は少ないので早めに確保を。
