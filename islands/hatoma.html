Hatoma Island鳩間島

A tiny, hushed coral island just north of Iriomote — barely fifty residents, almost no development, and famously clear water. Yaeyama's slow "island time" at its purest, reached by an often-cancelled open-sea ferry.西表のすぐ北に浮かぶ、ごく小さく静かなサンゴの島。住民はわずか約50人、開発もほとんどなく、透明な海で知られます。八重山の“島時間”を最も純粋に味わえる島で、外洋を渡る欠航の多いフェリーで訪れます。

Hatoma is about as quiet as an inhabited island gets — a single small village, coral-gravel lanes, near-zero traffic and water that shades from mint to deep indigo. You can walk the whole island in an hour or two. There's little to "do" in the usual sense, and that's exactly the appeal: a beach, a viewpoint, and the slow rhythm of a place time forgot. 鳩間は、有人島としては限りなく静かな島。小さな集落がひとつ、サンゴ砂利の路地、ほとんど無い交通量、そしてミントから濃紺へと移ろう海。島は1〜2時間で歩いて一周できます。いわゆる“やること”はほとんどありませんが、それこそが魅力。ビーチと展望、そして時が忘れたようなゆっくりした時間があります。

What to see見どころ

Hatoma Lighthouse sits on the island's high point, a short walk up from the port, with wide views over Iriomote's mountains and the surrounding reef. Yara-no-hama on the west coast is the largest beach — shallow, calm, turquoise, and good for a swim and a sunset. Mostly, though, the island itself is the sight: the village, the quiet, and the near-total lack of development. 鳩間島灯台は島の高台にあり、港から少し登れば、西表の山々と周囲のリーフを見渡せます。西海岸の屋良の浜は島最大のビーチで、浅く穏やかなターコイズの海。遊泳や夕日に最適です。とはいえ最大の見どころは島そのもの。集落、静けさ、そしてほとんど手つかずの風景です。

Getting there & the ferry — read this first行き方とフェリー（まず確認）

Ferries run from Ishigaki with Anei Kanko and Yaeyama Kanko Ferry, about 40–45 minutes direct (some services route via Uehara on Iriomote and take longer), with only around one or two round trips a day. Like the northern Iriomote routes, it crosses exposed open sea and is among the first to be suspended in strong wind or swell — extended multi-day suspensions have happened in the winter north-wind season. 船は石垣島から安栄観光・八重山観光フェリーで直行約40〜45分（西表・上原経由はさらに長い）。1日1〜2往復程度と少なめです。北の西表航路と同様、外洋を横切るため強風やうねりで最初に運休する路線のひとつ。冬の北風シーズンには数日以上の連続運休も起きています。

Practicalities実用メモ There are effectively no convenience stores, ATMs or vending — bring cash, water and food from Ishigaki. Camping is not allowed, so an overnight means booking one of the few small guesthouses. Get around on foot or by bike. And build in buffer time: don't plan a tight same-day connection, and re-check the ferry status the morning of travel. コンビニ・ATM・自販機は実質的にありません。現金・水・食料は石垣島から持参を。キャンプは不可のため、泊まるなら数少ない小さな民宿を予約。移動は徒歩か自転車で。予備時間も確保し、当日ギリギリの乗り継ぎは避け、出発の朝に運航状況を再確認しましょう。
Where to stay

Stay on Hatoma鳩間に泊まる

Just a few tiny, meals-included minshuku on this deeply quiet island — there are almost no shops or restaurants, so most stays feed you. Book ahead, and keep a buffer for the ferry.とても静かなこの島には、食事付きの小さな民宿が数軒だけ。商店や飲食店がほぼ無いため、多くの宿は食事付きです。早めに予約し、フェリーの予備日も確保を。
