Yaeyama Islands · 八重山（石垣島周辺）

Taketomi Island竹富島

A tiny, flat island of red-tile Ryukyu houses, white-sand lanes and water-buffalo carts — the closest of the Yaeyama islands to Ishigaki, and one of the easiest to reach.赤瓦の琉球家屋、白砂の路地、水牛車。石垣島から最も近い八重山の島で、いちばん気軽に渡れる島のひとつです。

Taketomi sits just ~6 km southwest of Ishigaki, so the crossing takes only about 10–15 minutes — which makes it the classic Yaeyama day trip. The whole island is small and flat, and its heart is a beautifully preserved traditional village: single-storey houses with red-tile roofs, coral-stone walls and sandy lanes, protected as a national Important Preservation District within Iriomote-Ishigaki National Park. 竹富は石垣島の南西わずか約6km。船で約10〜15分と近く、八重山の定番日帰り先です。島全体が小さく平坦で、その中心にあるのが美しく保存された伝統的集落。赤瓦屋根の平屋、サンゴの石垣、砂の路地が広がり、西表石垣国立公園内の国の重要伝統的建造物群保存地区に指定されています。

What to see見どころ

The village & buffalo carts. The signature experience is a slow water-buffalo cart tour through the lanes, the guide playing sanshin and singing Okinawan songs. It's a living village — people live here, so keep to public lanes and keep noise down. Kondoi Beach is the island's one swimming beach: wide, shallow and calm. Kaiji Beach is famous for its star-shaped sand (tiny foraminifera shells) you can sift from the shore — but swimming is not allowed there due to strong currents. 集落と水牛車。いちばんの体験は、三線を弾き島唄を歌うガイドとめぐる水牛車。人が暮らす“生きた集落”なので、私有地に入らず静かに。コンドイビーチは島唯一の遊泳ビーチで、広く浅く穏やか。カイジ浜（星砂の浜）は星の形の砂（有孔虫の殻）で有名ですが、潮流が強く遊泳は禁止です。

Getting there & the ferry行き方とフェリー

Ferries run from Ishigaki with Anei Kanko and Yaeyama Kanko Ferry, roughly every 30–60 minutes. Note the two operators' tickets are no longer interchangeable, so buy for the boat you board. This short, sheltered crossing is one of the more reliable Yaeyama routes and rarely cancels except in a direct typhoon — but rough seas can still stop it, so check our morning forecast before you go. 船は石垣島から安栄観光・八重山観光フェリーが約30〜60分間隔で運航。2社のチケットは共通ではないので、乗る船の会社で購入を。短く波の穏やかな航路で八重山では比較的欠航が少ない路線。台風直撃以外はめったに止まりませんが、荒天では欠航もあるため、出発前に朝の予報を確認しましょう。

Getting around島内の移動 The island is small and flat — rental bicycles are the easiest way around, plus buffalo-cart tours for the village. Most visitors come as day-trippers and the island empties after the last boat (~17:30); a few small minshuku let you stay for the quiet dusk and superb stargazing, but book ahead. 島は小さく平坦で、レンタサイクルが便利。集落は水牛車ツアーで。多くは日帰りで、最終便（17時半頃）後は静かに。夕暮れと満天の星を楽しむ小さな民宿もありますが、要事前予約です。
Where to stay

Stay on Taketomi竹富に泊まる

Most people day-trip, but staying overnight buys the quiet dusk and superb stargazing after the last boat — a few traditional minshuku and one luxury resort. Book well ahead.多くは日帰りですが、最終便後の静かな夕暮れと満天の星を味わうなら宿泊を。伝統的な民宿と上質なリゾートがあります。早めの予約を。
