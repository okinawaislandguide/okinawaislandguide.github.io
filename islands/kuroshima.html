Kuroshima Island黒島

A flat, heart-shaped island where cattle far outnumber people — quiet pasture, empty lanes, a sea-turtle research station and clear water. The easy-going cycling-and-snorkelling day trip of the Yaeyamas.牛が人よりずっと多い、平坦でハート型の島。静かな牧草地、人けのない道、ウミガメ研究所、澄んだ海。八重山でいちばん気楽な、サイクリング＆シュノーケルの日帰り先です。

Roughly heart-shaped and almost completely flat, Kuroshima is given over to open cattle pasture — there are said to be around ten cows for every resident. There's very little here in the best possible way: quiet lanes, grazing cows, a handful of beaches and some of the calmest snorkelling in Yaeyama. It's a nesting site for sea turtles, and the island's little research station is the place to learn about them. ほぼハート型で、ほとんど平坦な黒島は、その大半が放牧の牧草地。住民1人あたり牛が約10頭ともいわれます。ここには“いい意味で”ほとんど何もありません。静かな道、草を食む牛、いくつかのビーチ、八重山でも穏やかなシュノーケル。ウミガメの産卵地でもあり、小さな研究所でその生態を学べます。

What to see見どころ

The cattle and the quiet are really the point — cycle the flat lanes past open pasture (a lively Cattle Festival is held in late February). The Kuroshima Research Station is a small sea-turtle and reef research centre with aquariums and turtle rehabilitation. Quiet beaches like Nishi-no-hama offer gentle swimming and snorkelling with a good chance of turtles and reef fish. 牛と静けさこそが魅力。平坦な道を自転車で、放牧地の間をのんびり（2月下旬には賑やかな牛まつりも）。黒島研究所はウミガメとサンゴの小さな研究施設で、水槽やウミガメの保護を見学できます。西の浜などの静かなビーチでは、ウミガメや魚に出会える穏やかな遊泳・シュノーケルが楽しめます。

Getting there & the ferry行き方とフェリー

Ferries run from Ishigaki with Anei Kanko and Yaeyama Kanko Ferry (fares are the same on both), taking about 25–30 minutes. It's usually a reliable route, but like all Yaeyama crossings it's sea-condition dependent and winter north winds or typhoons can suspend it — check the morning forecast before you go. 船は石垣島から安栄観光・八重山観光フェリー（運賃は同額）で約25〜30分。比較的欠航が少ない路線ですが、八重山の航路はどれも海況次第で、冬の北風や台風で運休することも。出発前に朝の予報を確認しましょう。

Getting around & practicalities島内移動と注意点 Rent a bicycle at the port — the island is small and flat, and cycling is the way to see it. Note there are very few shops or eateries and no ATMs, so bring cash, water and anything you'll need for the day. It's a genuinely rural, slow place — most people come for the day. 港で自転車を借りるのが基本。小さく平坦な島はサイクリング向き。商店や飲食は非常に少なく、ATMもありません。現金・水・必要な物は持参を。本当にのどかでゆっくりした島で、多くは日帰りで訪れます。
Where to stay

Stay on Kuroshima黒島に泊まる

Most visitors come for the day, but a handful of small, quiet minshuku — several dive-friendly — let you stay for the slow evenings. Book ahead.多くは日帰りですが、静かな小さな民宿が数軒（ダイバー向けも）あり、のんびりした夜を過ごせます。早めの予約を。
