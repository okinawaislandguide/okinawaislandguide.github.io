Iriomote's recommended tour & activity operators西表のおすすめツアー・アクティビティ事業者

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Iriomote has the richest activity scene in the Yaeyamas — jungle kayaking to Pinaisara Falls, the Urauchi and Nakama mangrove river cruises, canyoning, snorkelling out to Barasu Island, and diving. These operators are cross-checked against the Taketomi Town tourism association and each one's official site, with what reviewers actually say. A few honest notes: most operators cluster around Uehara (north), with fewer on the Ohara (south) side; a rental car helps reach them; falls treks can be physically demanding; and Pinaisara Falls is now capped at 200 visitors a day (since 2025), so book the island's most popular tour early. English is limited except where noted. We aren't affiliated with any operator. 西表は八重山でいちばんアクティビティが充実——ピナイサーラの滝へのジャングルカヤック、浦内川・仲間川のマングローブ・リバークルーズ、キャニオニング、バラス島へのシュノーケル、ダイビング。以下は竹富町観光協会と各事業者の公式サイトで照合し、実際の口コミの評価点・気になる点を添えています。正直な補足：多くは上原（北）に集まり、大原（南）側は少なめ、移動はレンタカーが便利、滝トレックは体力を要することも、そしてピナイサーラの滝は2025年から1日200名の上限があるため島いちばんの人気ツアーは早めの予約を。英語対応は注記以外は限定的。当サイトは各事業者と提携していません。

Iriomote Adventure PiPi

Uehara (north) · ~1 min from Uehara Port · kayak · SUP · snorkel
One of the island's highest-volume, best-rated operators (around 4.8 from well over a thousand reviews) — guests praise the free photo-data service and the friendly, approachable guides, and note that all guides hold water-rescue certification, so it's welcoming to small kids and weak swimmers, with kayak-to-Pinaisara and Barasu snorkel among the menu. Honest note: its scale and popularity mean a high-volume operation with less of a boutique feel. Best for first-timers and families who want easy booking and polished service. It keeps an English-language website — the clearest foreign-guest orientation on the island.島でも屈指に口コミ件数が多く高評価の一軒（約4.8・千件超）——無料の写真データや、親しみやすいガイドが好評で、ガイド全員が水難救助資格を持つため小さな子どもや泳ぎが苦手な人にもやさしく、ピナイサーラへのカヤックやバラス島シュノーケルも。正直な点：規模と人気ゆえ“大人数運営”で、こぢんまり感は薄め。予約のしやすさと整ったサービスを求める初めての人・家族向け。英語サイトを持ち、島で最も外国人対応の下地がある一軒です。

Sorakaze

Uehara (north) · kayak · Pinaisara trek · Barasu snorkel
A hospitality-focused operator with genuinely high review volume and ratings across Google, Jalan and Tripadvisor — reviewers praise the guides' careful, thorough explanations, and the handmade Yaeyama-soba lunch is a repeated highlight, on combo mangrove-and-reef days. Honest note: the Barasu Island snorkel is tide-dependent (the sandbar only appears at low tide), so timing matters. Best for travellers wanting a smaller, warm team for a kayak + falls + snorkel day. English isn't evidenced, so a translation app helps.もてなし重視で、Google・じゃらん・Tripadvisorいずれも口コミ件数・評価が高い一軒——丁寧で分かりやすいガイド解説が好評で、手作りの八重山そばの昼食も繰り返し好評。マングローブ＋リーフの組み合わせ日程も。正直な点：バラス島のシュノーケルは干潮時のみ砂州が現れる潮次第なので、時間帯が重要。少人数で温かいチームと、カヤック＋滝＋シュノーケルを楽しみたい人向け。英語対応の根拠は無いため翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Ken Guide

Komi (south-east) · near Ohara side · kayak · trek · snorkel
A small owner-run outfit on the quieter south-east side (Jalan around 4.9 from ~200 reviews, and certified under the Taketomi Town guide ordinance) — reviewers repeatedly praise owner-guide "Ken" for his detailed explanations, answering every question and engaging well with children, with several calling it "the best time we spent on Iriomote." Honest note: being a one-guide operation, capacity and dates are limited and it books out. Best for couples and families wanting a personal small-group guide away from the busy north. English level isn't confirmed, so check.静かな南東側の小さなオーナー運営（じゃらん約4.9・約200件、竹富町の観光案内人条例の認定ガイド）——オーナーガイド“ケン”の丁寧な解説、どんな質問にも答える姿勢、子どもとの関わりが繰り返し好評で、「西表で一番の時間だった」との声も。正直な点：1人ガイドのため受け入れ数・日程は限られ、埋まりやすい。賑やかな北側を離れ、少人数で密なガイドを求めるカップル・家族向け。英語レベルは未確認のため要確認。

Sunnyday

Uehara (north) · kayak · Pinaisara trek · canyoning
A well-reviewed adventure operator (certified an "excellent" safety-standard operator by the prefectural public safety commission) — reviewers repeatedly praise the guides' high skill and rich plant-and-wildlife knowledge, which makes a hard tour feel safe and fun, and the handmade Yaeyama-soba lunch is a recurring favourite. Honest note: reviewers openly call the kayak-and-trek "hard" / physically tough — a fair warning for less-fit guests. Best for active travellers who want a knowledgeable guide on the falls routes. Likely Japanese-primary; confirm English.安全基準の“優良事業者”に認定された評価の高いアドベンチャー系——ガイドの技術と、植物・生き物の豊富な知識で、きついツアーも安全で楽しく感じられると繰り返し好評、手作りの八重山そば昼食も定番人気。正直な点：カヤック＋トレックは口コミでも率直に「きつい／体力を要する」とされ、体力に自信のない人には要注意。滝コースを知識豊富なガイドと楽しみたいアクティブ派向け。基本は日本語対応の見込みで、英語は確認を。

Mariud

Uehara (north) · kayak · Pinaisara & Gaeta Falls
A full-service outfit (gear, showers and shuttle included) also running a guesthouse and campground — reviewers highlight that guides take photos and hand you a same-day CD of them for free, and that they'll take you to quieter alternative spots (Gaeta Falls "with no other people," great for photos), with enjoyable nature commentary. Honest note: the full-day kayak-and-trek combos are physically demanding and weather-dependent. Best for travellers wanting an all-in outfit and a quieter falls alternative. Predominantly Japanese-guided.器材・シャワー・送迎込みのフルサービスで、ゲストハウスやキャンプ場も運営——ガイドが撮った写真を当日CDで無料でくれる点や、人の少ない別の滝（ゲータの滝は「他に誰もいない」と写真映え）へ案内してくれる点、楽しい自然解説が好評。正直な点：終日のカヤック＋トレックは体力を要し、天候次第。全部込みの装備と、静かな滝の選択肢を求める人向け。基本は日本語ガイドです。

Urauchigawa Kanko

Uehara (north) · Urauchi River cruise + falls trek
The sole operator of the flagship Urauchi River cruise — a sightseeing boat up Okinawa's largest river, which reviewers love for seeing the mangroves up close "like exploring the jungle by boat," accessible even to small children and people who can't kayak, with a praised free shuttle from Uehara Port. Honest note: the cruise itself is easy, but the onward trek to the Maryudu and Kanpire falls can be tough (some families with kids turn back), reviewers say prices have risen, and there are a few complaints about reception-staff attitude. Best for families and non-paddlers wanting the mangroves without the effort. Some foreign-guest handling; English guiding isn't confirmed.看板の浦内川クルーズを運航する唯一の事業者——沖縄最大の川を遡る遊覧船で、マングローブを間近に「船でジャングル探検」のようだと好評、小さな子どもやカヤックが無理な人でも参加でき、上原港からの無料送迎も好評。正直な点：クルーズ自体は楽ですが、その先のマリユドゥ・カンピレーの滝までのトレックはきつめ（子ども連れは引き返すことも）、料金が上がったという声や、受付対応への不満も少し。体力を使わずマングローブを楽しみたい家族・非パドル派向け。外国人対応の下地はあるものの英語ガイドは未確認。

Nakama River kayak (Kurage & south-side operators)

On the Ohara (south) side, operators such as Kurage run short, budget-friendly half-day (2–3 hour) kayak trips on the Nakama River — Japan's largest mangrove forest — close to Ohara Port. Reviewers like the accessible pricing and the tide-changing scenery. Honest note: it's strongly tide-dependent (timing dictates what you see), and the south side has fewer big-name operators than Uehara. Best for travellers staying or landing on the south side who want the Nakama mangroves without crossing the island. English varies by operator; confirm.大原（南）側では、海月（くらげ）などが、仲間川——日本最大のマングローブ林——で短めで手頃な半日（2〜3時間）カヤックを大原港近くで運航。手頃な価格と潮で変わる景色が好評です。正直な点：潮の影響が大きく（時間帯で見えるものが変わる）、南側は上原ほど有名事業者が多くありません。南側に泊まる/上陸する人が、島を横断せず仲間川のマングローブを楽しむのに向きます。英語は事業者次第、要確認。

Diving Team Unarizaki (Iriomote)

Uehara / Shirahama (north-west) · diving · lodging
A 30-plus-year veteran dive shop with attached lodging — a large jet-propulsion boat makes drift diving comfortable and safe, with access to the famous Ogan (Nakanokamishima) manta and big-fish point plus varied coral and macro sites, and thorough pre-dive instruction so non-swimmers can try. Honest note: the marquee outer points are conditions-dependent — the shop bases on north-side sites and only ventures to Ogan when the sea allows, so the big dives aren't guaranteed, and the current-prone sites suit experienced divers. Best for divers (beginner to veteran) after the island's signature big-ocean sites. Treat English as Japanese-primary unless confirmed.30年以上のベテランダイブショップで宿も併設——大型のジェット推進ボートでドリフトも快適・安全に、有名なオーガン（仲の神島）のマンタ・大物ポイントや多彩なサンゴ・マクロへ。体験も丁寧な事前講習で泳げない人も参加可。正直な点：目玉の外洋ポイントは海況次第で、北側を拠点にオーガンは条件が整ったときのみ——目玉ダイブは保証されず、流れの強い場所は経験者向け。島を代表する外洋ポイントを狙う初心者〜ベテランのダイバー向け。英語は確認できるまで日本語前提で。
