Taketomi · Where to stay

Where to stay on Taketomi — by travel style竹富の宿 — 旅のスタイル別

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Most people visit Taketomi as a day trip, but staying overnight is a quiet pleasure — after the last boat the preserved village empties of day-trippers, and the low light pollution makes for superb stargazing. Lodging is limited: a small number of traditional minshuku in the village (the three settlements are East, West and Naaji), plus one luxury resort. Always book ahead — rooms are few and fill fast. Get around by rental bicycle. 竹富は日帰りで訪れる人が多いですが、宿泊は静かな贅沢。最終便のあと、保存された集落から日帰り客が引き、光害が少ないため星空も見事です。宿は限られ、集落内の伝統的な民宿（集落は東・西・仲筋の3つ）が数軒と、上質なリゾートが1軒。予約は必ず事前に。部屋数が少なくすぐ埋まります。島内の移動はレンタサイクルで。

Takana Ryokan

Village centre · by the post office
The island's oldest inn, in the centre of the village — guests praise the generous, flavourful home-style meals made with local ingredients, and the historic character (the novelist Ryotaro Shiba stayed here while researching). Rooms range from simple shared-bath to renovated private units. It's an older traditional property, so the cheaper rooms are basic. Best for travellers who want authentic island character over modern polish. English is limited — confirm ahead; a translation app helps.集落の中心にある島最古の宿。口コミでは、地元食材の量たっぷりで滋味深い家庭料理と、歴史ある趣（作家・司馬遼太郎も取材で滞在）が好評。客室は共用バスのシンプルな部屋から改装済みの個室まで。古い伝統的な宿のため、安い部屋は簡素です。洗練より島らしい趣を求める人向け。英語は多くは通じないため要事前確認、翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Minshuku Izumiya

Village · on a buffalo-cart lane
A classic "flower inn" wrapped in bougainvillea on a lane where the water-buffalo carts pass with sanshin and island songs — homey, very modestly priced, and known for a complimentary bingata-costume photo experience for female guests. It's small (a handful of tatami rooms) with shared baths, and booking is by phone only. Best for budget travellers wanting a genuine old-style minshuku. English is minimal, so a translation app helps.ブーゲンビリアに包まれた昔ながらの“花の宿”。三線と島唄を響かせる水牛車が通る路地沿いにあり、家庭的でとても手頃。女性ゲストには紅型衣装の記念撮影体験も。小さく（畳の客室が数室）共用バスで、予約は電話のみ。手頃に本物の民宿らしさを味わいたい人向け。英語はごくわずかなので翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Yadoya Takenoko

West settlement · near Nishi-sanbashi (sunset pier)
A small, well-run guesthouse near the West Pier — a famous sunset spot — with consistently high review scores for service, cleanliness and both breakfast and dinner. All private rooms with private bath, free port pickup, and a garden with a hammock. It caps the number of groups per day, so it books out fast. Best for couples and adults wanting a calm, polished stay near the sunset. Confirm English when booking.夕日で有名な西桟橋近くの、小さくきちんと運営されたゲストハウス。サービス・清潔さ・朝夕食すべてで口コミ評価が高い一軒。全室バス付きの個室、港からの無料送迎、ハンモックのある庭も。1日の受け入れ組数を絞っているためすぐ満室に。夕日のそばで落ち着いた上質な滞在を求めるカップルや大人向け。英語対応は予約時に確認を。

Hotel Peace Island Taketomijima

Outside the village · ~20 min walk from port · ~15 min to Kondoi Beach
An all-cottage hotel set a little apart from the old village, with private-bath rooms, Ryukyu-plaster interiors, family rooms with washer/dryer, and a courtyard used for a nightly 21:00 stargazing session with complimentary cocktails. Being outside the preserved townscape, it's more "hotel" than village immersion. Best for families and travellers wanting private bathrooms and modern amenities. It offers online booking with some English support; confirm specifics.旧集落から少し離れた全棟コテージのホテル。バス付き客室、琉球漆喰の内装、洗濯乾燥機付きのファミリールーム、毎晩21時の星空観賞（カクテル無料）の中庭も。保存地区の外にあるため、集落に浸るというより“ホテル”寄り。個室バスやモダンな設備を求める家族や旅行者向け。オンライン予約可で英語もある程度対応、詳細は確認を。

Hoshinoya Taketomi Island

Inland resort "village" · shuttle from the settlements
Taketomi's luxury choice — a purpose-built village of 48 freestanding villas with red-tile roofs and coral-plaster walls that echo the island's architecture, with a pool, spa, Okinawan cuisine, a nightly lounge and stargazing. Guests love the privacy and design. It's a self-contained resort somewhat apart from village life, and priced far above everything else on the island. Best for a splurge or honeymoon. As a Hoshino Resorts property it has the island's strongest English support.竹富の上質系。赤瓦屋根とサンゴ漆喰の壁で島の建築を映した48棟の独立ヴィラからなる“集落”で、プール・スパ・沖縄料理・夜のラウンジ・星空観賞も。プライバシーとデザインが好評。集落の暮らしからは少し離れた独立型リゾートで、価格帯は島でも突出。特別な滞在や新婚旅行向け。星野リゾート運営で、島でいちばん英語対応が手厚い一軒です。
