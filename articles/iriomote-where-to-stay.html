Where to stay on Iriomote — by travel style西表の宿 — 旅のスタイル別

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Iriomote is a big island, and where you stay matters — lodging clusters around two ferry ports. Uehara (north) has the most stays and is closest to the famous jungle and waterfalls, but its ferry is cancelled far more often (open, exposed sea); when it stops, boats reroute to Ohara plus an island bus. Ohara (south) is quieter for lodging but the more reliable arrival port. We note each stay's nearest port below. A rental car is effectively essential (buses are sparse) — book it, and your room, well ahead. 西表は大きな島で、どこに泊まるかが重要です。宿は2つのフェリー港の周辺に集まります。上原（北）は宿が最も多く、名高いジャングルや滝に近い一方、フェリーの欠航が多め（外洋で風波に弱い）。運休時は大原発＋島内バスに振り替わります。大原（南）は宿は少なめですが、到着港としては欠航に強いのが利点。以下では各宿の最寄り港を明記します。レンタカーはほぼ必須（バスは少ない）。車も宿も早めの予約を。

Hoshino Resorts Iriomote Hotel

Uehara side (north) · beachfront · ~10 min by car from Uehara Port
The island's largest, beachfront resort, right behind Tsukigahama Beach — guests praise the spacious sea-facing rooms (you hear the waves), the attentive staff and a strong breakfast (the black-sugar French toast gets singled out), plus a spa and easy snorkel-tour booking. On the honest side, some rooms show their age and the Wi-Fi/mobile signal can be patchy. Best for travellers wanting a full-service resort base. As the island's big Hoshino-brand hotel, it's the most foreigner-friendly, with English support.月ヶ浜のすぐ後ろに立つ、島最大のビーチフロントリゾート。口コミでは、海に面した広い客室（波の音が聞こえる）、行き届いたスタッフ、充実の朝食（黒糖フレンチトーストが評判）、スパやシュノーケルツアーの手配のしやすさが好評。一方で、一部客室は古さを感じ、Wi-Fiや電波が不安定なことも。フル装備のリゾートを拠点にしたい人向け。星野ブランドの大型ホテルで、島でいちばん外国人に優しく英語対応もあります。

Iriomote Jungle Hotel Painumaya

Uehara side · set inland in the jungle · car/shuttle access
A nature hotel set deep in the jungle with no surrounding buildings — guests love the immersive setting and stargazing, the "Iriomote buffet" of local ingredients, and the attentive night nature tours, which make it popular with families. On the honest side, the buildings feel dated, dinner variety draws some criticism, and buses are very few, so confirm ferry/bus times before arrival. Best for nature- and activity-focused stays. It's used to overseas guests, though not a luxury service level.周囲に建物のないジャングルの中の自然派ホテル。口コミでは、没入感ある環境と星空、地元食材の“西表ビュッフェ”、丁寧なナイトネイチャーツアーが好評で、家族連れにも人気。一方で、建物は古さを感じ、夕食の品数に不満の声も。バスが非常に少ないため、到着前にフェリー・バスの時間を確認しましょう。自然や体験重視の滞在向け。海外客に慣れていますが、サービスは高級路線ではありません。

Kanpira-so

Uehara village · ~2–3 min walk from Uehara Port · shop opposite
An unbeatable-value inn right in Uehara village, a couple of minutes from the port with a supermarket across the road — guests like the location and price (from around ¥4,000, room-only), rooms with or without private bath, and a sociable atmosphere that draws divers and repeat regulars. The building is old but kept clean, and it's no-frills. Best for divers and budget travellers who want to be steps from the port and shops. English is limited; a translation app helps.上原集落のど真ん中、港から徒歩2〜3分でスーパーも向かいという好立地の宿。口コミでは立地と価格（素泊まり約4,000円〜）、バス・トイレ付き／なしを選べる客室、ダイバーや常連が集う社交的な雰囲気が好評。建物は古いものの清潔で、気取りなし。港と商店のすぐそばに泊まりたいダイバーや予算重視の人向け。英語は多くは通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Irumote-so

Uehara side · hilltop, ~15 min walk from Uehara Port
One of Okinawa's few remaining youth hostels, on a grassy hilltop with sea views toward Hatoma — guests praise how clean it is, the sociable vibe (shared awamori and rooftop stargazing), and kind owners who'll drive guests in the rain or for shopping. Air-con and coin laundry included. The hilltop makes cycling harder, and footsteps can carry between guests at night. Best for solo travellers and backpackers wanting a social, cheap base. Backpacker mix means more foreigner-friendly than a typical minshuku, but keep English expectations modest.沖縄でも数少ない現存ユースホステルのひとつ。鳩間を望む草地の高台にあり、口コミでは清潔さ、社交的な雰囲気（泡盛の回し飲みや屋上の星空観賞）、雨の日や買い物に車を出してくれる親切なオーナーが好評。エアコン・コインランドリー付き。高台のため自転車はやや大変で、夜は物音が伝わることも。社交的で手頃な拠点を求める一人旅・バックパッカー向け。バックパッカーが多く一般的な民宿より外国人に慣れていますが、英語は過度に期待しないのが無難です。

Minshuku Ikedaya

Ohara side (south) · ~5 min walk from Ohara Port · near shops
A home-style minshuku best known for its food — the owner is a former professional chef, and daily fresh-caught fish (snapper sashimi, grilled rabbitfish), generous portions and seasonal local ingredients (summer mango, winter Ryukyu wild boar and mangrove crab) bring guests back. It's on the south side, a short walk from Ohara Port and rental-car offices — handy when the Uehara ferry is cancelled. Facilities are modest; you come for the meals and location. English is limited; confirm ahead.料理で名高い家庭的な民宿。オーナーは元プロの料理人で、その日獲れの新鮮な魚（フエダイの刺身、アイゴの塩焼き）、たっぷりの量、季節の地元食材（夏はマンゴー、冬は琉球イノシシやマングローブガニ）を目当てにリピーターも。南側の大原港・レンタカー店から徒歩すぐで、上原便が欠航のときに便利。設備は素朴で、料理と立地が魅力。英語は多くは通じないため要事前確認。
