Kohama's recommended marine & activity operators小浜のおすすめマリンショップ・アクティビティ事業者

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Kohama is resort-oriented, and the independent activity scene is small — essentially two Kohama-resident marine shops plus a veteran Ishigaki operator that offers a Kohama-departure option. The draw is snorkelling, usually combined with the "phantom island" (Hamajima) sandbar and the Panari/Kuroshima reefs. A couple of honest notes: SUP, kayak and SUP-yoga on Kohama are mostly sold through a booking portal (kohama-tour.com) using contracted guides rather than a single resident brand, and the two big resorts (Haimurubushi and Risonare Kohamajima) run in-house activities mainly for their guests. These operators are cross-checked against the Taketomi Town tourism association. English is limited across the small shops. We aren't affiliated with any operator. 小浜はリゾート志向で、独立系のアクティビティは小規模——実質小浜在住のマリンショップ2軒と、石垣拠点で小浜発も選べるベテラン1軒です。魅力はシュノーケルで、たいてい「幻の島」（浜島）の砂州やパナリ／黒島のリーフと組み合わせます。正直な補足を2つ：小浜のSUP・カヤック・SUPヨガは、単一の在住ブランドというより予約ポータル（kohama-tour.com）が契約ガイドで催行するものが中心。そして2大リゾート（はいむるぶし・リゾナーレ小浜島）は主に宿泊者向けの館内アクティビティを運営します。以下は竹富町観光協会と照合。小さな各店の英語対応は限定的です。当サイトは各事業者と提携していません。

🐠

mine (Kohamajima Asobi Senmonten)

Kohama village · snorkel · phantom island · photos
A Kohama-resident all-round marine shop (Google and Jalan both around 4.6) — reviewers cite the kind, attentive staff, the free photos sent afterwards and strong value, snorkelling around Kuroshima, Panari and the phantom island, with kids accepted from about age one. Honest note: one 3-star review felt the snorkel guiding lacked instruction and wasn't worth a premium price, and another noted a 30-plus-minute wait despite a booking; advance reservation is essentially required as it fills up. Best for families wanting an all-round, photo-friendly outing. No English-support statement found; treat as Japanese-first.小浜在住の総合マリンショップ（Google・じゃらんとも約4.6）——親切で気配りのあるスタッフ、後日届く無料写真、コスパの良さが好評で、黒島・パナリ・幻の島のシュノーケル、1歳頃から参加可。正直な点：ある3つ星の口コミは「シュノーケルの指導が物足りず割高に感じた」、別の4つ星は「予約済みでも30分以上待った」と。人気で埋まるため事前予約はほぼ必須。写真も楽しめる総合的な外出を求める家族向け。英語対応の記載は見つからず、日本語前提で。

HaiSai (Kohamajima Umiasobi Shop)

Kohama village · snorkel · phantom island · SUP
A Kohama-resident snorkel shop that ranks among the island's top choices for couples and families on Jalan — reviewers praise the owner-guide's roughly 20 years of local experience and the small-group (max about eight) personalised handling suited to beginners, kids and seniors, plus the freedom to combine the phantom island and Panari in one day from Kohama itself. Honest note: it needs a minimum of two people to run and day-tour pricing starts around ¥8,800; no consistent complaint surfaced. Best for families and beginners wanting a flexible half- or full-day snorkel from Kohama. English isn't advertised — confirm in advance.小浜在住のシュノーケル店で、じゃらんではカップル・家族向けの上位常連——オーナーガイドの約20年の地元経験と、初心者・子ども・年配に合う少人数（最大8名ほど）の対応、そして小浜発で幻の島とパナリを1日に自由に組める点が好評。正直な点：催行は最少2名、日帰り料金は約8,800円〜、一貫した不満は見当たらず。小浜発で柔軟に半日〜1日のシュノーケルを楽しみたい家族・初心者向け。英語は明示されておらず、事前確認を。

Sea Technico

Ishigaki-based · Kohama-departure option · snorkel · diving
A long-established operator (since 1981) that runs a dedicated Kohama-departure tour combining snorkel, intro diving and the phantom island in one trip, so you can choose an Ishigaki or a Kohama start. Honest note: it's primarily an Ishigaki-based operation offering Kohama pickups, so scheduling depends on Ishigaki logistics, and Kohama-specific review volume is limited. Best for divers or those wanting an established outfit with a Kohama option. Assume Japanese-first; confirm English.1981年創業の老舗で、シュノーケル＋体験ダイビング＋幻の島を1回にまとめた小浜発ツアーを設定——石垣発／小浜発を選べます。正直な点：本来は石垣拠点で小浜ピックアップを提供する形のため、日程は石垣側の都合次第で、小浜発に絞った口コミ件数は限られます。ダイバーや、小浜発も選べる老舗を求める人向け。日本語前提で、英語は確認を。
