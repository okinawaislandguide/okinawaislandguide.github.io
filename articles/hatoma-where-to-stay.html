Hatoma · Where to stay

Where to stay on Hatoma — by travel style鳩間の宿 — 旅のスタイル別

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Hatoma is tiny and deeply quiet — one small village, almost no shops or restaurants and no ATM — so nearly every stay is a small family-run minshuku where meals are included or provided on-site; self-catering isn't really practical. All lodging is walkable from the port. The ferry cancels frequently in rough seas, so keep a buffer day and re-check the status the morning you travel. Book ahead, and bring cash from Ishigaki. 鳩間はとても小さく静かな島——集落はひとつ、商店や飲食店はほとんど無く、ATMもありません。そのためほぼ全ての宿が、食事付き（または館内提供）の小さな家族経営の民宿で、自炊は現実的ではありません。宿はどれも港から徒歩圏。フェリーは荒天でよく欠航するため、予備日を確保し、出発の朝に運航状況を再確認しましょう。早めの予約と、石垣島での現金の用意を。

Pension Maitozoe

Village, near the port · meals included
A long-established inn praised above all for its food and its completely private rooms (not shared or dorm). Dinner centres on fresh local-caught fish — simmered, fried and as sashimi — with a signature home-made "duck soba" using the inn's own farm-raised duck, eaten in a south-facing oceanfront dining room as the sea changes through the day. One quirk: the sleeping quarters and dining hall are a few minutes' walk apart. Best for those wanting private rooms and the best food on the island. English is very limited; a translation app helps.何よりも料理と、完全個室（相部屋・ドミトリーでない）で評価される老舗の宿。夕食は地元で獲れた新鮮な魚が中心——煮付け・揚げ・刺身に、自家養殖の合鴨を使った名物「鴨そば」も。海に面した南向きの食堂で、移ろう海を眺めながら味わえます。ひとつの特徴として、就寝棟と食堂は数分歩いて離れています。個室と島いちばんの食事を求める人向け。英語はほとんど通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Minshuku Idafuni

~5 min walk from the port · meals included
A homey, welcoming minshuku (look for the glass-float buoys out front) run by a sanshin player and singer who performs at the Hatoma music festival — so a stay comes with authentic Okinawan music and easy "yuntaku" evenings with the host. Home-cooked meals; small and intimate (around ¥5,500 with two meals). Best for travellers who want island-music culture and real host interaction over privacy. English is very limited; a translation app helps.家庭的で温かい民宿（軒先の石垣の浮き玉が目印）。オーナーは鳩間島音楽祭でも演奏する三線奏者・唄者で、本場の沖縄音楽と気さくな“ゆんたく”の夜が楽しめます。家庭料理付きで、小さくアットホーム（2食付き約5,500円）。プライバシーより島の音楽文化とホストとの交流を求める人向け。英語はほとんど通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Minshuku Marudai

On the road toward the lighthouse hill · meals included
The island's oldest and most atmospheric inn — a 100-year-plus traditional red-tile Okinawan house that appeared in the TV drama "Ruri no Shima." Home-cooked meals and a nostalgic, deeply quiet, authentic island-life feel (around ¥5,500 for a night with three meals). By design it's very basic and rustic — thin walls, simple facilities — so it suits travellers who want the classic old-Okinawa minshuku experience over comfort. English is very limited; a translation app helps.島でいちばん古く趣のある宿——築100年を超える伝統的な赤瓦の沖縄家屋で、ドラマ「瑠璃の島」にも登場しました。家庭料理付きで、懐かしくとても静かな、本物の島暮らしの雰囲気（3食付き1泊約5,500円）。造りはとても素朴で簡素——壁は薄く設備もシンプル——なので、快適さより昔ながらの沖縄の民宿らしさを求める人向け。英語はほとんど通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Minshuku Adanashi

Village, short walk from the port · casual · dinner at the adjoining cafe
The most casual, budget-friendly base on the island — a relaxed, backpacker-friendly guesthouse with six rooms, where dinner is served at the adjoining cafe "Poppo," so you can book room-only and still eat well on-site. Room-only is around ¥4,000 (small summer air-con surcharge; breakfast from about ¥800). Best for budget and solo travellers who want the lowest price and a social feel rather than full ryokan service. English is likely very limited; a translation app helps.島でいちばん気軽で手頃な拠点——のんびりバックパッカー向けの6室のゲストハウスで、夕食は併設カフェ「ぽっぽ村」で提供されるため、素泊まりでも館内でしっかり食べられます。素泊まり約4,000円（夏はエアコン加算あり、朝食約800円〜）。旅館的なサービスより、最安値と社交的な雰囲気を求める予算重視・一人旅向け。英語はほとんど通じない見込みで、翻訳アプリが役立ちます。
