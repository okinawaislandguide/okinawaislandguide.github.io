Aka & Geruma · Where to stay

Where to stay on Aka & Geruma — by travel style阿嘉・慶留間の宿 — 旅のスタイル別

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Aka is a small, quiet island, so always book ahead — it's essential in summer and wise year-round, as rooms are few and there's nowhere to fall back on if you arrive without a reservation. Most stays are family-run minshuku, pensions and guesthouses rather than big hotels, so what really differs is the style of stay — filter the list below. 阿嘉は小さく静かな島なので、宿は必ず事前予約を。夏は特に必須、それ以外も基本は予約を。部屋数が少なく、無予約で来ると泊まれないこともあります。宿の多くは大型ホテルではなく家族経営の民宿・ペンション・ゲストハウス。違うのは泊まる「スタイル」です。下のリストで絞り込んでください。

Where the stays are. Almost all accommodation is in Aka village, which is compact and walkable — everything below is within roughly 3–10 minutes of Aka Port. Maebama Beach is right by the village; the long, famous Nishibama Beach is about a 15–20 minute walk (or a quick cycle) to the north. Geruma is joined to Aka by the Aka Bridge but is tiny, with essentially no lodging of its own — most visitors stay on Aka and cross over to Geruma for its exceptionally clear water during the day. 宿はどこに？ 宿泊はほぼすべて阿嘉集落にあり、集落はコンパクトで徒歩圏。以下はどれも阿嘉港から徒歩およそ3〜10分です。前浜（マエバマ）ビーチは集落のすぐそば、有名な長いニシバマビーチは北へ徒歩15〜20分（自転車ならすぐ）。慶留間（ゲルマ）は阿嘉大橋で阿嘉とつながりますが非常に小さく、宿はほぼありません。多くの人は阿嘉に泊まり、日中に透明度抜群の慶留間へ渡ります。

Guest House Tomisato (Tourato)

Aka village · ~10 min from port · 1 min to Maebama Beach
A homely family minshuku a minute from Maebama Beach, run by a working fisherman — guests rave about the dinner above all: fresh fish the owner landed that day, served in generous helpings, with lively evening "yuntaku" chats over awamori. Rooms are simple Japanese-style with shared facilities, about a ten-minute walk from the port. Best for travellers who want genuinely local island cooking and a sociable, home-like stay rather than privacy or polish. English is limited, but with a translation app communicating is no trouble.前浜ビーチから徒歩1分、現役の海人（漁師）が営む家庭的な民宿。口コミで何より評判なのが夕食で、その日オーナーが獲った新鮮な魚が量たっぷりに並び、夜は泡盛を片手に賑やかな“ゆんたく”も。客室はシンプルな和室・共用設備で、港から徒歩約10分。プライバシーや洗練より、正真正銘の島の家庭料理と社交的な雰囲気を求める人向け。英語は多くは通じませんが、翻訳アプリがあればやり取りに支障はありません。

West Coast

Aka village · right on Maebama Beach · ~8 min from port
A log-house inn right in front of Maebama Beach — guests love the beachfront setting, the ocean-view open-air bath, the big home-cooked spreads shared around the table (with awamori to follow) and the easy, sociable common areas and stargazing. Rooms are simple bunk-bed style with clean shared bathrooms, and many guests are here to dive. Best for beach lovers and divers happy with shared, no-frills, sociable lodging rather than private comfort. English is limited, but a translation app makes it easy.前浜ビーチの目の前に建つログハウス風の宿。口コミでは、ビーチ最前列のロケーション、オーシャンビューの露天風呂、みんなで囲む手作りの大皿料理（食後は泡盛）、居心地のよい共有スペースと星空が好評。客室はシンプルな2段ベッド中心で、共用バスは清潔。ダイビング目的の宿泊客も多め。個室の快適さより、気取らず社交的にビーチとダイビングを楽しみたい人向け。英語は多くは通じませんが、翻訳アプリがあれば大丈夫です。

Minshuku Sakubaru

Aka village · central · ~3 min from port
A traditional, spotlessly kept minshuku in the centre of Aka village, about three minutes from the port and an easy walk to Maebama Beach — guests praise the calm, home-like feel, how clean everything is, and the friendly couple who run it (a characterful dad and a warm, cheerful mum). All Japanese-style rooms with shared shower and toilet; room-only stays are available. Best for first-timers and solo travellers wanting a quiet, affordable, no-fuss base. English is limited, but a translation app makes things easy.阿嘉集落の中心にある、掃除の行き届いた昔ながらの民宿。港から徒歩約3分、前浜ビーチも徒歩圏。口コミでは、落ち着いた家庭的な雰囲気、隅々まで清潔なこと、営むご夫婦（キャラの濃いお父さんと、明るく優しいお母さん）の人柄が好評。全室和室でシャワー・トイレは共同、素泊まりも可。静かで手頃、気楽に泊まりたい初めての人や一人旅向け。英語は多くは通じませんが、翻訳アプリがあれば問題ありません。

Guest House Smile

Aka village · ~9 min from port
One of Aka's most highly rated stays — a guesthouse that feels more like a small pension. Guests single out how clean and comfortable it is, the kind owner and the tasty home cooking, and the rooms come with their own private shower and toilet (unusual at this price). There's also a dormitory and a shared mini-kitchen, with room-only or two-meal plans, about nine minutes' walk from the port. Best for budget travellers who still want privacy and a spotless, well-run base rather than a party atmosphere. English is limited, but a translation app makes communicating easy.阿嘉でも評価がとても高い一軒。ゲストハウスというより小さなペンションのような趣きで、口コミでは、清潔で快適なこと、親切なオーナー、美味しい家庭料理が評価されています。客室はこの価格帯では珍しく個室にシャワー・トイレ付き。ドミトリーや共用ミニキッチンもあり、素泊まり・2食付きが選べます。港から徒歩約9分。賑やかさより、手頃でもプライバシーと清潔さ・きちんと運営された宿を求める人向け。英語は多くは通じませんが、翻訳アプリがあればやり取りは簡単です。

Pension Seasir (Marine House Seasir Aka)

Aka village · ~5 min from port · dive resort
A dive-resort pension run by Seasir, one of Kerama's established operators — guests praise the well-run diving (small groups, beginner- and solo-friendly, a boat set up for easy entry), the clean Japanese- and Western-style rooms, the rooftop jacuzzi and the on-site restaurant and gear-wash area. Being a larger operation it feels less intimate than a family minshuku, but it's the easiest option if you don't speak Japanese: the shop operates in English (as well as Chinese and Korean), so it's a popular, comfortable base for international divers.慶良間の老舗ダイビング事業者シーサーが運営する、ダイビング宿泊付きのペンション。口コミでは、少人数で初心者や一人参加にもやさしいダイビング（エントリーしやすい設計のボート）、清潔な和室・洋室、屋上ジャグジー、併設レストランや器材の洗い場が高評価。家族経営の民宿ほどのアットホーム感はないものの、日本語が不安でも安心なのが強み。ショップは英語（中国語・韓国語も）対応で、海外からのダイバーに人気の快適な拠点です。

Guest House AKIZATA

Aka village · ~3 min from ferry terminal · 15 min to Nishibama
A relaxed guesthouse three minutes from the ferry terminal, with a rooftop terrace, a shared self-catering kitchen and BBQ corner, an on-site food wagon, plus 24-hour showers, a sand-rinse tap and a washing machine — genuinely handy after a beach day (Nishibama is about a 15-minute walk). Five simple air-conditioned rooms with fridge and microwave; it's more of a self-service base than a meals-included minshuku. The crowd skews international, so it's an easy, sociable pick for overseas travellers and English is no problem.フェリーターミナルから徒歩3分の気楽なゲストハウス。屋上テラス、自炊できる共用キッチンとBBQコーナー、敷地内のキッチンワゴン、24時間使えるシャワー・砂流し用の水道・洗濯機まで揃い、ビーチ帰りに便利（ニシバマまで徒歩約15分）。冷房付きのシンプルな客室5室に冷蔵庫・電子レンジ。食事付きの民宿というより自炊型の拠点です。宿泊客は海外からの旅行者が多く、英語も問題なく通じる社交的な一軒。

Blue Sweets Hanamuro on the beach

Aka village · beachfront · ~6 min from port
Aka's upscale choice — a tiny beachfront resort of just a few suites, where guests single out the private rooftop hot tubs with sunset and sea views, the outdoor pool, and the generous, beautifully presented dinners with attentive service. It's an adults-oriented, quiet retreat rather than a family or backpacker base. As the island's priciest stay with very few rooms, it books out early, so reserve well ahead — best for couples wanting comfort and a room with a view over a sociable minshuku. Some English is spoken; it's worth confirming any specific needs when you book.阿嘉では数少ない上質系。客室わずか数室の小さなビーチフロントのリゾートで、口コミでは、夕日と海を望むプライベートな屋上露天風呂、屋外プール、美しく盛られたボリュームある夕食と行き届いたサービスが特に評価されています。大人向けの静かな隠れ家で、家族連れやバックパッカー向けではありません。島でいちばんの高価格帯かつ部屋数が少なく早く埋まるため、早めの予約を。社交的な民宿より、快適さと眺めのよい客室を求めるカップル向け。簡単な英語なら通じますが、細かな要望は予約時に確認を。
