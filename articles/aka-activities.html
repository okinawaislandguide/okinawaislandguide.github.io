Aka's activity scene is small and diving-led — a few dive shops in Aka village that also run boat snorkel, and one kayak/SUP specialist. These are drawn from the operators on the Zamami Village guidemap, with what reviewers actually say. How it usually works: most run two sessions a day — a morning departure timed to the first high-speed boat's arrival and an afternoon one. Two honest notes: boat snorkel is a side menu of the dive shops rather than a snorkel-only brand, and whale watching isn't run from Aka — you join a Kerama tour via Zamami in winter (a short inter-island ferry). Book ahead; trips are weather-dependent, and we aren't affiliated with any operator. 阿嘉のアクティビティは小規模でダイビング中心——阿嘉集落の数軒のダイブショップ（ボートシュノーケルも実施）と、カヤック/SUP専門店が1軒です。以下は座間味村ガイドマップ掲載の事業者を基準に、実際の口コミの評価点・気になる点を添えています。基本の流れ：多くは1日2回催行——高速船1便の到着に合わせたAM出発と午後出発。正直な補足を2つ：ボートシュノーケルはシュノーケル専門店ではなくダイブショップの一メニュー、そしてホエールウォッチングは阿嘉発が無く、冬は座間味経由（島間の短いフェリー）で参加します。予約は事前に、催行は海況次第。当サイトは各事業者と提携していません。

Marine House Seasir Aka

Aka village · diving · boat snorkel · near the port
The island's largest and most structured operator (a Kerama dive chain since 1983) — reviewers and guests note the small-group intro dives (max two guests per instructor), a purpose-built dive boat that's easy for women and older guests to get in and out of, and short 5–15 minute runs to 70+ nearby sites; it placed 2nd for "Best Diving Shop" at the 2023 Marine Diving Awards. Honest note: it's the biggest, most commercial shop on the island, so it's best if you want an established, well-organised operation rather than an intimate owner-run feel; public review volume is thin. As a group with an English website it likely handles English — confirm for the Aka branch.島でいちばん大きく体制の整った事業者（1983年〜の慶良間のダイブチェーン）——少人数の体験ダイビング（インストラクター1名につき最大2名）、女性や年配でも乗り降りしやすい専用ボート、5〜15分で行ける70以上のポイントが好評で、2023年マリンダイビングアワードのベストダイビングショップ2位。正直な点：島で最も規模が大きく“商業的”な一軒なので、アットホームさより整った運営を求める人向け（第三者の口コミ件数は少なめ）。英語サイトを持つグループのため英語対応の下地はありそうですが、阿嘉店については要確認。

Dive Service Karahai

Aka village · small, owner-guided · free pickup
A husband-and-wife shop praised in reviews for careful, unhurried small-group guiding that suits beginners and photographers — one snorkeller noted the guide taught practical basics and stayed attentive in the water so a first-timer felt safe, with free pickup to the port or your guesthouse. Honest note: it's a genuinely small couple-run operation, so capacity and scheduling are limited — best for beginners and small groups wanting a relaxed, personal dive, not large parties. No English support found; assume Japanese and confirm ahead.夫婦で営むショップで、口コミでは、初心者や写真好きに合う丁寧でのんびりした少人数ガイドが好評——あるシュノーケル利用者は、ガイドが基本（髪の結び方など実用的なコツ）を教え、水中でも付きっきりで見てくれて初めてでも安心だったと。港や宿への無料送迎も。正直な点：本当に小さな夫婦経営のため受け入れ数・日程は限られ、大人数より、のんびり個人的に潜りたい初心者・少人数向け。英語対応は確認できず、日本語前提で事前確認を。

Dive Service Cucururu

Aka village · homey, small-group · comfortable boat
A homey, couple-run shop that guests describe as warm and unhurried, with a comfortable private boat ("Cucururu-go") fitted with a toilet and shower for onboard comfort. Honest note: it caters specifically to people who want slow, low-volume diving in a small group, so it's best for relaxed or photo divers rather than a high-tempo or large-group day. The shop says many of its guests are from overseas, so there may be some foreign-guest experience — confirm English ahead.夫婦で営むアットホームな一軒で、温かくのんびりした雰囲気と、トイレ・シャワー付きで快適な自前のボート「ククルル号」が好評。正直な点：少人数でゆっくり少なめに潜りたい人向けの店なので、高テンポや大人数より、のんびり・写真派に。海外からのゲストも多いと店側は述べており、外国人対応の下地はありそうですが、英語は事前に確認を。

EARTHSHIP kerama

Aka village · sea kayak · SUP · turtle snorkel
Aka's dedicated kayak/SUP and snorkel specialist, well rated by reviewers (about 4.9 from ~24 reviews on a booking platform) for high turtle and coral encounter rates, kind attentive guides good with children and beginners, and 30-plus free photos per tour. Honest note: one reviewer said the course details weren't clear before booking, so confirm the plan. Important for overseas visitors: the operator's own site states it runs in Japanese only and declines foreign-national guests because its insurance doesn't cover them — so it suits Japanese-speaking travellers; English speakers wanting to paddle should arrange kayaking on Zamami instead.阿嘉唯一のカヤック/SUP・シュノーケル専門店で、口コミ評価も高く（予約サイトで約4.9・約24件）、ウミガメやサンゴの高い遭遇率、子どもや初心者にやさしい丁寧なガイド、1回30枚以上の無料写真が好評。正直な点：コース内容が予約前に分かりにくかったという声もあるので、プランは確認を。海外からの方へ重要：本店は公式サイトで「日本語のみ対応・外国籍のお客様は保険適用外のためお断り」と明記しています。日本語で参加できる旅行者向けで、英語で漕ぎたい方は座間味でのカヤックをご検討ください。
