Tokashiki · Operators

Tokashiki's recommended marine & activity operators渡嘉敷のおすすめマリンショップ・アクティビティ事業者

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Tokashiki has the fullest activity scene of the Kerama islands, most of it around Aharen Beach — diving, boat snorkel, sea kayak, a seasonal night stargazing tour, and winter humpback whale watching (roughly late January–mid March). These operators are cross-checked against the Tokashiki tourism association and each one's official site, with what reviewers actually say. How it usually works: most run two sessions a day — a morning departure timed to the first high-speed boat's arrival from Naha, and an afternoon one. One honest note: many "Tokashiki day tour" products sold online actually depart from Naha — the operators below are island-based. Book ahead; trips are weather-dependent, and we aren't affiliated with any operator. 渡嘉敷は慶良間でもいちばんアクティビティが充実し、その多くが阿波連ビーチ周辺——ダイビング、ボートシュノーケル、シーカヤック、季節限定の星空ナイトツアー、そして冬のザトウクジラのホエールウォッチング（おおむね1月下旬〜3月中旬）。以下は渡嘉敷村観光協会と各事業者の公式サイトで照合し、実際の口コミの評価点・気になる点を添えています。基本の流れ：多くは1日2回催行——那覇からの高速船1便の到着に合わせたAM出発と午後出発。正直な補足：ネットで売られる「渡嘉敷日帰りツアー」の多くは実は那覇発で、以下は島拠点の事業者です。予約は事前に、催行は海況次第。当サイトは各事業者と提携していません。

Marine House Aharen

Aharen Beach · diving · boat snorkel · day packages
The island's top-rated dive-and-snorkel operator on the diving-review sites (around 4.2 stars) — reviewers praise the boat snorkel, where the captain and guide take you to good spots with a high sea-turtle encounter rate, and the friendly, at-home atmosphere for beginners; all-in day packages include a shuttle to both beaches and shower/locker use. Honest note: the attached lodging is budget and basic, so it's best for a beginner snorkel/soft-dive plus a cheap beachfront stay. It keeps an English (and Chinese) website — phone support is Japanese only, English via the contact form — so confirm your needs. (Listed via Okinawa Story and review sites rather than the tourism association directory.)ダイビング系口コミサイトで島トップ評価（約4.2）のダイブ＆シュノーケルの一軒——口コミでは、船長とガイドが良いポイントへ案内しウミガメ遭遇率の高いボートシュノーケルや、初心者にやさしいアットホームな雰囲気が好評。両ビーチへの送迎やシャワー・ロッカー利用込みの日帰りプランも。正直な点：併設の宿は低予算で簡素なので、初心者シュノーケル／体験ダイビング＋手頃なビーチ前泊向け。英語（中国語）サイトを備え、電話は日本語のみ・英語は問い合わせフォーム——要確認で。（観光協会の掲載ではなく、おきなわ物語や口コミサイトで確認）

Diving & Minshuku Shima Ashibi

Village side · small-group diving · lodging
A small, well-reviewed dive shop with lodging (on the tourism association directory), run by a female owner who is a former canoe guide, boat captain and dive instructor — reviewers praise the capped daily bookings and small-group, personal service, the warm hospitality on land and in the water, free dive photos, and how comfortable it is for solo travellers. Honest note: no real complaints surfaced, but the small scale means limited capacity, so book ahead. Best for solo and small-group divers who want intimate guiding. English support isn't confirmed, so a translation app helps.宿を併設する評価の高い小さなダイブショップ（観光協会掲載）で、元カヌーガイド・船長・ダイブインストラクターの女性オーナーが運営——口コミでは、1日の受け入れを絞った少人数の丁寧な対応、陸でも海でも温かいもてなし、無料の水中写真、一人旅でも居心地がよい点が好評。正直な点：目立った不満は無い一方、小規模ゆえ受け入れ数は限られるので早めの予約を。少人数で密なガイドを求める一人旅・少人数のダイバー向け。英語対応は未確認のため翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Seafriend

Aharen Beach · large dive operation · stay-and-dive
One of the prefecture's larger dive operations (13 instructors, 4 boats; on the tourism association directory), right on Aharen Beach with its own lodging and izakaya — reviewers say the attentive safety briefings reassured first-time boat-snorkellers, staff film guests underwater, and the handmade buffet breakfast varied nicely over multi-night stays. Honest note: guests mention thin walls, and the shared shower has no separate changing area (you change inside the shower). Best for divers wanting a large, structured operation with a beach base. English support isn't clearly evidenced, so confirm ahead.県内でも規模の大きいダイブ事業者（インストラクター13名・ボート4隻／観光協会掲載）で、阿波連ビーチの目の前に自前の宿と居酒屋も——口コミでは、丁寧な安全説明で初めてのボートシュノーケルでも安心、水中撮影サービス、連泊でも変化する手作りビュッフェ朝食が好評。正直な点：壁が薄い、共同シャワーに脱衣スペースが無い（シャワー内で着替え）という声も。ビーチ拠点で大きく体制の整った運営を求めるダイバー向け。英語対応の明確な根拠は無いため事前確認を。

Tokashiku Marine Village — marine activities

Tokashiku Beach · turtle snorkel · beachfront resort
The marine desk of the beachfront hotel on turtle-famous Tokashiku Beach — reviewers single out the very high wild sea-turtle encounter right in front of the hotel (staff will point out where the turtles are), and the room-to-beach convenience with showers, a gear-wash and a drying area on the path. Honest note: the buildings are old (unit baths and tatami, a "training-camp" feel) and food variety is limited. Best for families wanting turtle snorkel with beachfront lodging. English support isn't evidenced, so confirm ahead. (The first-source Tokashiku-beach shop on the association directory is Marine House Gakiya; this is the resort's own marine offering.)ウミガメで名高いとかしくビーチ前のホテルのマリン拠点——口コミでは、ホテル目の前での野生アオウミガメの高い遭遇率（スタッフがいる場所を教えてくれる）、部屋からビーチへの動線（道中にシャワー・器材洗い・乾燥スペース）が好評。正直な点：建物は古め（ユニットバス・畳の“合宿所”感）、食事の品数は限られます。ビーチ前泊でウミガメシュノーケルを楽しみたい家族向け。英語対応は未確認のため事前確認を。（観光協会掲載のとかしくビーチの店は「マリンハウス我喜屋商店」で、これはホテル自体のマリン提供）

Islands Trip

Aharen Beach · guided sea kayak + snorkel
The island's only guided half-day sea-kayak tour (on the tourism association directory, and featured on the national parks site), paddling from Aharen Beach to an uninhabited island — very well reviewed (around 4.9 from ~30 reviews) with kayak novices saying the guide support made it easy, "stunning" water and summit views, gentle staff, and a shower and transfer included. Honest note: none of substance surfaced. Best for active travellers wanting a kayak + snorkel combo and an uninhabited-island landing. It offers an online experience tour, but spoken English isn't confirmed — confirm ahead.島で唯一のガイド付き半日シーカヤックツアー（観光協会掲載、国立公園サイトでも紹介）で、阿波連ビーチから無人島へ漕ぎます——口コミ評価が高く（約4.9・約30件）、カヤック初心者もガイドの支えで問題なし、「息をのむ」海と山頂からの眺め、やさしいスタッフ、シャワー・送迎込みが好評。正直な点：目立った短所は見当たらず。カヤック＋シュノーケルや無人島上陸を楽しみたいアクティブ派向け。オンライン体験ツアーもありますが当日の英語は未確認のため事前確認を。

Quest — night stargazing & nature

Aharen · seasonal (summer) · night tour
A seasonal night tour under Tokashiki's dark Kerama skies — a beach stargazing course for constellations and Milky Way photos, and a forest-trail course that adds fireflies and bioluminescent plankton. Honest note: there's little independent review volume to judge it by, and the guide (the café's site manager) is only on the island in summer, so it's a seasonal add-on rather than a year-round option. Best for overnight guests wanting a low-light-pollution stargazing extra. Run in Japanese with no English support mentioned, so a translation app helps. (Not on the tourism association directory; via the operator's own site.)渡嘉敷の暗い慶良間の夜空を歩く季節限定のナイトツアー——星座や天の川を撮るビーチの星空コースと、ホタルや夜光虫も加わる森のトレイルコース。正直な点：独立した口コミが少なく評価材料は乏しめで、ガイド（カフェの現地責任者）は夏のみ在島のため、通年ではなく季節の“追加”です。灯りの少ない星空を楽しみたい宿泊者向け。日本語で運行し英語対応の記載は無いため翻訳アプリが役立ちます。（観光協会掲載ではなく事業者公式サイト経由）

Kujiland

Tokashiki-based · whale watching · ~late Jan–Mar
A Tokashiki-based whale-watching boat running winter humpback tours (roughly February–March) under the island association's responsible-viewing rules — reviews of Tokashiki whale trips describe powerful encounters (breaching, spy-hopping), good scenery, and lunch-plus-island-stroll combos. Honest note: it's strictly seasonal (winter–spring only), and weather can cancel a trip; reserve the evening before. Best for staying on Tokashiki and joining an island-based winter tour. English isn't evidenced, so confirm ahead.渡嘉敷を拠点にする冬のホエールウォッチング船（おおむね2〜3月）で、島の協会の責任あるルールのもと運航——渡嘉敷のホエールツアーの口コミでは、ブリーチングやスパイホップなど迫力ある遭遇、景観、昼食＋島内散策の組み合わせが好評。正直な点：冬〜春の完全な季節運行で、荒天では中止も。前日夜までに予約を。渡嘉敷に泊まって島発の冬ツアーに参加したい人向け。英語は根拠が無く、事前確認を。

Tokashiki Whale-Watching Association

Tokashiki · the framework for winter tours
The island's whale-watching association (a non-profit set up in 2023, on the village's official site) is the conservation-first framework its member boats follow: morning-only departures, keeping at least 50 m away and slowing within 300 m, and a restricted season timed to when whale numbers peak. You book through a member operator (such as Kujiland), and reviewers report experienced captains and a high encounter rate. Honest note: it's a governing body, not a single bookable brand — and the short, morning-only season is by design. Good context for booking responsibly.島のホエールウォッチング協会（2023年設立の非営利・村公式サイト掲載）は、加盟船が守る保全優先の枠組みです：午前のみの出航、50m以上離れ300m圏内では減速、クジラの数がピークの時期に限った短いシーズン。予約は加盟事業者（例：クジランド）へ。口コミでは経験豊富な船長と高い遭遇率が報告されています。正直な点：単一の予約ブランドではなく統括団体で、午前のみ・短期シーズンは意図的な設計。責任ある予約のための参考に。
