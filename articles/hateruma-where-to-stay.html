Where to stay on Hateruma — by travel style波照間の宿 — 旅のスタイル別

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

An overnight on Hateruma is worth it for the star skies — but the ferry is one of Yaeyama's most cancellation-prone routes, so book flexibly and keep a buffer day. Lodging is a small pool of family-run minshuku and a backpacker guesthouse; most offer free port pickup if you phone ahead. Note that several are room-only (no meals) and the island has very few shops or restaurants, so check the meal plan when you book, and bring cash. Reserve well ahead — inventory is tiny and the best places fill within hours. 波照間の宿泊は星空のために価値がありますが、フェリーは八重山でも屈指に欠航しやすい路線。柔軟に予約し、予備日を確保しましょう。宿は家族経営の民宿が数軒とバックパッカー宿。多くは電話連絡で港送迎に対応します。素泊まり（食事なし）の宿も多く、島には商店や飲食店がほとんど無いため、予約時に食事の有無を確認し、現金も用意を。宿は少なく人気宿は数時間で埋まるので、早めの予約を。

Pension Sainantan

Above Nishihama Beach · ocean-view · free port pickup · meals included
The island's most sought-after stay, on the hill directly above Nishihama Beach — all rooms have an ocean view, there's a rooftop for stargazing, and rooms come with a private unit-bath and air-con. Two meals are included, which solves the "nowhere to eat dinner" problem common on remote islands: swim, rinse off, walk to your room. On the honest side it's extremely hard to book (reserve ~3 months ahead) and insects can get into rooms. Best for those who want the beachfront location. English is limited; a translation app helps.島でいちばん人気の宿。ニシ浜のすぐ上の高台にあり、全室オーシャンビュー、屋上は星空観賞に、客室はバス・トイレ付き＋エアコン。2食付きで、離島にありがちな「夕食難民」問題も解決。泳いで、流して、部屋へ。一方で予約が非常に取りにくく（3か月ほど前から）、虫が入ることも。ビーチ前のロケーション重視の人向け。英語は多くは通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Minshuku Hoshizora-so

Village · free port pickup · room-only
A long-established village inn marked by blooming bougainvillea — guests praise the clean, private rooms, each named after a constellation, fitting the island's stargazing draw. It's calm and quiet with little group socialising, so it's a good pick for privacy. Note it's currently room-only (no meals), so arrange dinner in the village. Best for travellers who want a calm, private base over a party atmosphere. English is limited; a translation app helps.ブーゲンビリアが咲く、集落の老舗の宿。口コミでは、清潔で個室のプライバシーが好評で、各部屋は星座の名前——星空の島にぴったりです。落ち着いて静かで、団体的な交流は少なめなので、プライバシー重視の人に。現在は素泊まり（食事なし）のため、夕食は集落で。賑やかさより静かで個室の拠点を求める人向け。英語は多くは通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Minshuku Kedamoto-so

Village edge · free port pickup · room-only
A simple, very cheap minshuku on the village outskirts (room-only from around ¥2,500) — guests appreciate the kind, helpful owner who's good with local recommendations, and there's a women-only toilet/bath that solo female travellers find comfortable. Facilities are basic and meals aren't offered, so you self-cater or eat in the village. Best for budget solo travellers, especially women. English is limited; a translation app helps.集落の外れにある、シンプルでとても手頃な民宿（素泊まり約2,500円〜）。口コミでは、地元情報に詳しく親切なオーナーが好評で、女性専用のトイレ・浴室があり女性の一人旅も安心。設備は簡素で食事提供はないため、自炊か集落で。予算重視の一人旅、とくに女性向け。英語は多くは通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

🌏

Guesthouse Nami

Quiet north settlement · free port pickup · dorm-style
The island's most social backpacker option — dormitory-style, in a quiet settlement in the north, with a lively communal "yuntaku" where travellers gather and excellent value. There's an optional evening plate of home-made tofu and sashimi, and the cooking gets warm mentions. On the honest side it's a base rate without meals, with many repeat guests, so first-timers can feel it's an established regulars' scene; facilities are basic and shared. Best for sociable budget and solo travellers. English is limited; a translation app helps.島でいちばん社交的なバックパッカー向けの宿。北の静かな集落にあるドミトリー形式で、旅人が集う賑やかな“ゆんたく”とコスパの良さが魅力。夜には手作り豆腐と刺身のプレート（任意）もあり、料理も好評。一方で基本は素泊まりでリピーターが多く、初めてだと常連の輪に感じることも。設備は簡素な共用。社交的で予算重視の一人旅向け。英語は多くは通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。
