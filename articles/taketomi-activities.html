Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Taketomi is a preserved-village culture island rather than a marine-activity hub — most people come as a half-day trip from Ishigaki (a 10–15 minute ferry). The signature experience is the water-buffalo cart tour through the traditional red-tile village with a sanshin-singing guide; beyond that there's a glass-bottom boat for a quick coral peek and bicycle rentals for reaching Kondoi and Kaiji beaches. These are cross-checked against the Taketomi Town tourism association, with what reviewers actually say. Two honest notes: buffalo-cart fares have risen and one operator's cart is currently suspended (see below), so confirm on the day; and an e-assist bike is worth it for the island's small slopes. We aren't affiliated with any operator. 竹富はマリンの拠点というより、保存集落の“文化の島”——多くは石垣島から半日（フェリー10〜15分）で訪れます。いちばんの体験は、三線を弾き島唄を歌うガイドとめぐる水牛車で赤瓦の伝統集落へ。ほかに、サンゴを手軽に覗くグラスボートと、コンドイ・カイジ浜へ行くレンタサイクル。以下は竹富町観光協会と照合し、実際の口コミの評価点・気になる点を添えています。正直な補足を2つ：水牛車の料金は上がり、片方の事業者の水牛車は現在休業中（下記）なので当日確認を。そして島の小さな坂には電動アシスト自転車が便利です。当サイトは各事業者と提携していません。

Taketomi Kankou Center

Village · water-buffalo cart · bike rental · free port shuttle
The larger of the island's buffalo-cart operators — reviewers love that the guide narrates the island's history in dialect and sings shimauta with a sanshin as the cart winds past hibiscus and red-tile roofs ("very lovely"), and the one-stop convenience (port pickup, and you can rent bikes here too). Honest note: the most common complaints are that it feels "too commercial / systematised" with bigger crowds; one reviewer found the ride short and objected to the guide whipping the buffalo; and a "¥3,000 too expensive" comment (fares vary and have risen — check on the day). Its glass-boat is currently marked closed. Best for first-timers wanting the classic ride plus one-stop logistics. Narration is in Japanese.島の水牛車事業者のうち大きい方——ガイドが方言で島の歴史を語り、三線で島唄を歌いながら、ハイビスカスや赤瓦の間を進むのが「とても素敵」と好評で、ワンストップの便利さ（港送迎、ここで自転車も借りられる）も。正直な点：最も多い声は「商業的・システム化されすぎ」で人が多いこと、乗車が短く感じ、水牛に鞭を打つのが気になったという声、そして「3,000円は高い」という声（料金は変動・値上がり傾向＝当日確認を）。併設のグラスボートは現在休止中。定番の乗車＋ワンストップを求める初めての人向け。案内は日本語です。

Nitta Kanko

Village · bike rental (buffalo cart currently suspended)
Historically the originator of Taketomi's buffalo-cart tourism and the smaller, more old-fashioned operator — reviewers valued the veteran elderly guides who play sanshin and sing folk songs with humour, and the very slow, gentle ~30-minute ride (not scary for kids), with a more traditional, less mass-market feel. Honest note: per the tourism association, its water-buffalo cart is currently suspended — it's operating rental bicycles only, so don't plan around the cart; and there are recurring complaints of brusque staff. Best for a bike rental now, or the old-school cart if it resumes. Narration in Japanese.もともと竹富の水牛車観光の元祖で、小さく昔ながらの事業者——三線を弾き民謡をユーモアたっぷりに歌うベテランの年配ガイドや、とてもゆっくり優しい約30分の乗車（子どもも怖くない）、大衆的すぎない伝統的な雰囲気が好評でした。正直な点：観光協会によると水牛車は現在休業中で、レンタサイクルのみの営業——水牛車を当てにしないこと。加えて、接客がそっけないという声も繰り返しあります。今はレンタサイクルとして、あるいは水牛車が再開すれば昔ながらの体験に。案内は日本語です。

Nansei Kanko (glass-bottom boat)

Taketomi Port area · ~30 min · no swimwear needed
The island's dedicated glass-bottom-boat operator (one of Yaeyama's oldest tourism firms and the first to run glass boats here) — a ~30-minute ride, around ¥1,200, needing no swimwear, so it's accessible for kids and seniors. Reviewers say the coral is genuinely impressive (some rate it above Kabira Bay — "like a green canyon"), with clownfish sightings and enthusiastic guides. Honest note: it's a short, passive sightseeing ride — glass-boat viewing, not a snorkel or dive scene — so it can underwhelm anyone expecting a real marine-activity day. Best for a quick coral peek added to a village visit. Commentary in Japanese; confirm the weather/tide-dependent schedule.島唯一のグラスボート事業者（八重山でも古い観光会社で、この地でグラスボートを最初に運航）——約30分・約1,200円、水着不要で子どもや年配にもやさしい。口コミではサンゴが本当に見事（川平湾より上と評する声も＝「緑の渓谷のよう」）、クマノミも見え、ガイドも熱心。正直な点：短く受け身の遊覧＝グラスボート鑑賞で、シュノーケルやダイビングではないため、本格的なマリンを期待すると物足りないことも。集落見物にサンゴをちょい足しする用途向け。案内は日本語、天候・潮次第の時刻は確認を。

Maruhachi Rental Cycle

Village · bike rental · free port shuttle
An association-listed bike shop with a free port shuttle — reviewers note the staff are helpful, give you a tourist map and explanation on arrival, and that walk-ins are easy ("if the ojisan's there, he'll lend you one on the spot"); standard bikes around ¥800 for two hours, with e-assist available (worth it for the island's small slopes). Honest note: it's a small operation, so it can be informal and staff-dependent for walk-ins at busy times. Best for casual day-trippers who want to reach Kondoi and Kaiji beaches quickly. Process is simple and visual, manageable for non-Japanese speakers.観光協会掲載で港無料送迎付きの自転車店——口コミでは、到着時に観光マップと説明をくれる親切なスタッフや、飛び込みでも借りやすい点（「おじさんがいればその場で貸してくれる」）が好評。普通車で2時間約800円、電動アシストもあり（島の小さな坂に便利）。正直な点：小規模なので、混雑時の飛び込みはやや不定・スタッフ次第なことも。コンドイ・カイジ浜へさっと行きたい気軽な日帰り向け。手続きは簡単で視覚的、日本語が不安でも対応しやすいです。

Tomori Kanko (rental cycle)

Village · largest bike fleet · free port shuttle
The island's largest bike fleet with a convenient free port shuttle — many reviewers found the staff polite and the operation well run, and the scale means bikes are usually available. Honest note: service reviews are genuinely split (some report courteous staff, others curt service), and its chartered-taxi option is currently suspended. Best for groups or peak-time visitors who want assured bike availability. The rental process is straightforward for non-Japanese visitors, though there's no explicit English support.島最大の自転車台数と便利な港無料送迎——多くの口コミでスタッフが丁寧で運営もしっかり、台数が多く借りやすいと好評。正直な点：接客の評価は割れており（丁寧という声もあれば、そっけないという声も）、貸切タクシーは現在休止中。台数を確実に確保したいグループや繁忙期の来訪者向け。貸出手続きは分かりやすいものの、明確な英語対応の記載はありません。
