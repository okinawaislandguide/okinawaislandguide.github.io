Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Zamami's activities are almost all boat-based marine trips from Zamami village — diving, boat snorkel and kayak/SUP over the clear "Kerama Blue" water, plus a guided nature walk and, in winter (January–March), humpback whale watching. These are recommended operators drawn from those listed on the Zamami Village Tourism Association's own directory, with what reviewers actually say — the good and the honest caveats. How it usually works: most run two sessions a day — a morning departure timed to the first high-speed boat's arrival from Naha, and an afternoon one — so even a day-tripper can usually fit in a session. Book ahead; trips depend on the weather, and we aren't affiliated with any operator. 座間味のアクティビティは、ほぼすべて座間味集落発のボートによるマリン——透き通る「ケラマブルー」でのダイビング・ボートシュノーケル・カヤック/SUPに、自然ガイド、そして冬（1〜3月）のホエールウォッチング。以下は、座間味村観光協会の掲載事業者を基準にしたおすすめの事業者と、実際の口コミの評価点・正直な気になる点です。基本の流れ：多くは1日2回催行——那覇からの高速船1便の到着に合わせたAM出発と、午後出発——なので日帰りでも1回は参加しやすいです。予約は事前に、催行は海況次第。当サイトは各事業者と提携していません。

Piece

Zamami village · beginners · boat snorkel · try dives
The dive shop with the strongest online reviews on the island — visitors highlight the friendly, responsive staff, a high turtle-encounter rate ("I finally met the turtle I'd longed for"), and free GoPro photos and video sent afterwards at no extra charge. Honest note: it's beginner- and experience-dive-focused (about 90-minute sessions over two sites), so it's not aimed at advanced divers wanting many dives, and it's popular enough to book out. Best for first-timers who want turtles plus free photos and video. Japanese-first; confirm English in advance.島のダイブ店の中でもネット口コミが最も多い一軒——親切で対応の良いスタッフ、ウミガメ遭遇率の高さ（「念願のウミガメに会えた」）、そして後日無料で送られるGoProの写真＆動画が好評です。正直な点：初心者・体験ダイビング中心（約90分・2ポイント）で、本数を潜りたい上級者向けではなく、人気のため埋まりやすいです。ウミガメと無料の写真・動画目当ての初めての人に。基本は日本語対応なので英語は事前に確認を。

Joy Joy

Zamami village · veteran shop (since 1983)
The island's oldest dive shop (since 1983) — reviewers praise the kind, safety-first instructors and how well they look after nervous young children (a family with a 2nd-grader was grateful), with free showers and changing rooms afterwards. Honest note: a reviewer or two found the staff interaction a little reserved, and visibility suffers on rough-weather days. Best for those who want a veteran local operator. It's Japanese-first, so confirm English in advance.島最古のダイブショップ（1983年〜）。口コミでは、親切で安全第一のインストラクターや、緊張しがちな小さな子どもへの配慮（小2連れの家族が感謝）が好評で、ダイブ後のシャワー・更衣室も無料。正直な点：スタッフの対応がやや淡白と感じた声や、荒天日は透明度が落ちるという声も。老舗の地元店を求める人向け。基本は日本語対応なので英語は事前に確認を。

TOIRO (Umiasobi Toiro)

Zamami village · charter-style · female guide
A small, charter-style operator and one of the island's few female-guided shops — paced to the guest and explicitly welcoming to people nervous about swimming, snorkel beginners, women and families with small children, guiding beyond the guarded zone at Ama Beach. Honest note: the private, small-group model means limited capacity, and it's snorkel- and experience-dive-focused (full fun-diving is by prior arrangement), so it's not the pick for divers wanting many dives. The shop's own site says it can handle English enquiries and bookings (by email or LINE); confirm your needs.少人数・貸切スタイルで、島では数少ない女性ガイドの一軒——ゲストのペースに合わせ、泳ぎが不安な人・シュノーケル初心者・女性・小さな子ども連れを歓迎し、阿真ビーチの遊泳区域の外まで案内します。正直な点：貸切・少人数のため受け入れ数は限られ、シュノーケルや体験ダイビング中心（本格ファンダイブは要相談）なので、本数を潜りたい人には不向き。英語は問い合わせ・予約に対応と自社サイトに記載（メール/LINE）。要相談で。

Coral Divers Club

Zamami village · fun dives · experienced
A veteran fun-dive operator that uses roughly 40 years of local tidal data to time dives to where the fish gather, with a signature drift-diving style that starts and ends in calm water, 100+ dive points and student discounts. Honest note: it has almost no online reviews and an older-style web presence, so there's little third-party feedback to go on — best for repeat or experienced divers who value deep local knowledge over a polished online profile. No English support found; confirm in advance.約40年分の潮の動きのデータを使い、魚が集まるタイミングにダイブを合わせるベテランのファンダイブ店。流れの無い所で始めて終える独自のドリフトスタイル、100以上のポイント、学割も。正直な点：ネット上の口コミがほとんど無く、サイトも古風で、第三者の評価材料は乏しめ——洗練された発信より地元の知見を重視するリピーター・経験者向け。英語対応は確認できず、事前確認を。

Dive Center NO-Y

Zamami village · dive-and-stay · winter whale watching
A dive shop with its own lodging (Pension Takatsuki) a 3–5 minute walk from the port, handling fun and experience diving plus winter whale watching — handy for a stay-and-dive trip, with fully equipped rooms. Honest note: it's a long-running, traditional dive-inn (since 1994) with very few online reviews, so there's little third-party feedback — best for divers who want to stay and dive in one place rather than a large modern operation. No English support found; confirm in advance.自前の宿（ペンション高月）を港から徒歩3〜5分に構えるダイブショップ。ファン・体験ダイビングに加え冬はホエールウォッチングも扱い、泊まって潜るのに便利で客室も一通り揃います。正直な点：1994年からの昔ながらのダイブ民宿で、ネット口コミは非常に少なく評価材料は乏しめ——大規模店より、1か所で泊まって潜りたい人向け。英語対応は確認できず、事前確認を。

Zamami Sailing

Zamami village · catamaran snorkel & dive cruise
Snorkel and dive cruises on "Tingara," a stable, low-rocking catamaran with a toilet and a hot shower — reviewers like the comfortable big-boat platform and the experienced family crew who tailor the day to mixed skill levels. Honest note: a genuine negative review felt repeat regulars were prioritised over first-timers and mentioned some less-experienced instructors, limited dive points, slow gear changes and higher prices than other shops (it's a small sample, so weigh it). Best for those wanting a comfortable big boat. Confirm English in advance.安定して揺れの少ないカタマラン「ティンガーラ」（トイレ・温水シャワー付き）で行くシュノーケル＆ダイブクルーズ——快適な大型船と、混在するレベルに合わせてくれる経験豊富な家族クルーが好評。正直な点：常連が初めての客より優先される印象、経験の浅いインストラクター、ポイントが限られる、器材交換が遅い、他店より割高、という辛口レビューも（少数意見のため参考程度に）。快適な大型船を求める人向け。英語は事前に確認を。

Cat's

Zamami village · near-port turtle snorkel · gear rental
A near-port operator — its stable cruiser "MOTHER" takes up to 27 and leaves about a minute from the port — offering turtle-focused boat snorkel, gear and island-vehicle rental, and attached lodging. Honest note: it's on the tourism association's directory, but we couldn't find independent guest reviews, so treat the specifics as the operator's own description and confirm details directly. It offers LINE / WhatsApp / WeChat contact, which suggests some ability with overseas guests, though English guiding isn't confirmed. Best for a convenient near-port turtle snorkel with gear and mobility sorted.港のすぐそば（大型で安定したクルーザー「MOTHER」は最大27名、港から約1分）で、ウミガメ狙いのボートシュノーケル、器材や島内移動手段のレンタル、併設の宿も。正直な点：観光協会の掲載事業者ですが、独立した口コミが見つからなかったため、内容は事業者側の説明として直接確認を。LINE/WhatsApp/WeChat対応があり海外客の受け入れにある程度慣れている様子ですが、英語ガイドは未確認。器材と移動も一度に整う、港近くの手軽なシュノーケル向け。

noah

Zamami village · SUP & kayak · small groups
One of the best-reviewed paddle operators — visitors repeatedly praise the small groups (around four people), the attentive, kid-friendly guides (helping children spot hermit crabs, giving nervous snorkelers flotation help), reliable sea-turtle sightings and the guide photos included in every plan. Honest note: no substantive complaints surfaced; weather can force a change of spot, but guides adapt to still-good coral and fish. Best for families and first-timers wanting a relaxed small-group SUP/kayak and snorkel. Confirm English in advance.パドル系でも指折りに口コミの良い一軒——少人数（4名前後）、子どもにやさしく面倒見の良いガイド（ヤドカリ探しを手伝う、不安な人には浮力サポート）、高確率のウミガメ、そして全プランに付くガイド写真が繰り返し好評です。正直な点：目立った不満は見当たらず、天候でポイント変更はあってもサンゴや魚の良い場所へ柔軟に対応。少人数でのんびりSUP/カヤック＋シュノーケルを楽しみたい家族・初心者向け。英語は事前に確認を。

Kerama Kayak Center

Zamami village · kayak + snorkel · most English reviews
An established kayak-and-snorkel operator with the best English-review footprint on the island — international visitors praise guides who show lots of marine life (including snorkelling with sea turtles), take great photos, give thorough first-timer instruction, and pick you up by car when you arrive. Honest note: some guests booked expecting an English-speaking guide but found the guide explained in Japanese and translated, so on-tour English isn't guaranteed even though email is handled in English. Best for a well-run, relatively English-friendly kayak + snorkel half-day.島でいちばん英語の口コミが多い、実績あるカヤック＆シュノーケルの一軒——海外からの利用者が、たくさんの海の生き物（ウミガメと泳ぐシュノーケルも）を見せてくれるガイド、上手な写真、初心者への丁寧な指導、到着時の車での送迎を高く評価。正直な点：英語ガイドを期待して予約したら、実際は日本語＋通訳だった、という声もあり、メールは英語対応でも当日の英語は保証されません。運営がしっかりして比較的英語に強めの、カヤック＋シュノーケル半日向け。

Nature Land Kayaks

Zamami village · sea kayak & SUP · owner-guided (since 2001)
Okinawa's first sea-kayak specialist (since 2001), run by an owner couple — reviewers praise the careful SUP instruction, reliable sea-turtle sightings and thoughtful, attentive service (shuttle included; well reviewed even by a school-trip group), with small groups (max six per guide) and kids welcome from about age six. Honest note: no real negatives surfaced in findable reviews; it's an owner-couple operation and likely Japanese-primary, so confirm English in advance. Karaful Wave — nature guide

Asa · guided nature walks · land-based whale watching
A resident nature guide (Tsubocchi, on the island since 1994, a former officer of the village whale-watching association) whose walks reviewers love for the engaging, story-driven explanations of the island's plants, birds and wildlife — "more fun than expected," and popular with both kids and adults (rated around 4.7 from about 90 reviews on a booking platform; figure as of 2026, please confirm). In winter he's known for land-based whale watching, spotting humpbacks from the Takatsukiyama observatory — no seasickness, but it depends on weather and the season (Jan–Mar) and it's a distant hillside view, not a boat encounter. Honest note: no real negatives surfaced; the site is Japanese-only, so English support is limited — confirm ahead. Best for nature and birdwatching lovers and families wanting an easy, educational outing.座間味在住（1994年〜、村ホエールウォッチング協会の元役員）のネイチャーガイド“つぼっち”。島の植物・野鳥・生き物を、物語のように面白く解説する散策が口コミで人気で、「期待以上」と子どもも大人も楽しめると好評（予約サイトで約4.7・約90件。2026年時点・要確認）。冬は高月山展望台からの陸のホエールウォッチングで知られ、船酔いは無いものの天候と時期（1〜3月）次第で、あくまで高台からの遠望（船での接近ではありません）。正直な点：目立った短所は見当たらず。サイトは日本語のみで英語対応は限定的なため事前確認を。自然・野鳥好きや、気軽で学びのある外出を求める家族向け。

Zamami Whale-Watching Association

Zamami village · humpback whale watching · Jan–Mar
From January to March (the 2026 season runs Jan 8–Mar 25), the village association coordinates humpback whale watching: land spotters on the mountain observatory confirm whales before the boats launch and radio-guide them, which reviewers credit for frequent close breaching and a very high sighting rate, with less seasickness than the long crossing from Naha and a responsible-viewing ethos (a 100 m distance rule). Honest note (from real reviews): whales are never guaranteed — one disappointed reviewer saw only a single distant whale in poor weather ("it's luck"); in seas above about 2.5 m the boat pitches and motion-sick-prone people will likely be sick; and a trip can be cancelled outright if no whales are visible at departure. It's a coordinating body — you book one of about 11 member boats (which vary), and only two, Escort (with an underwater microphone to hear whale song) and Venus (a larger two-deck boat), flag English support.1〜3月（2026年は1/8〜3/25）、村の協会がホエールウォッチングを統括します。山の展望台の陸上見張りがクジラを確認してから船を出し無線で誘導するため、口コミでも近くでのブリーチングが多く遭遇率が非常に高いと評判で、那覇からの長い航海より船酔いも少なめ、100m以上離れる責任あるルールも。正直な点（実口コミ）：クジラは保証されず、悪天で遠くに1頭だけだった「残念！！」という声も（“運”次第）。波高がおよそ2.5mを超えると船は大きく揺れ、船に弱い人はまず酔います。出航時に展望台からクジラが見えなければツアー自体が中止になることも。統括団体のため予約は約11隻の加盟船から1隻（船により差）で、英語対応を掲げるのはエスコート（水中マイクでクジラの声）とビーナス（大型2階建て）の2隻のみです。
