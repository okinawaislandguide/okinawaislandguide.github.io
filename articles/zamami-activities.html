Things to do on Zamami — activities & marine shops座間味のアクティビティ — マリンショップ一覧

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Zamami is one of Japan's best marine playgrounds — clear "Kerama Blue" water, coral and sea turtles, all reached by boat from Zamami village. Below are the island's diving shops, boat-snorkel and kayak/SUP operators and nature guides, plus winter humpback whale watching (January–March). Sea conditions rule everything here, so book ahead, keep a buffer day, and check our morning ferry forecast — the same rough seas that cancel the ferry also cancel the boats. 座間味は日本屈指のマリンフィールド——透き通る「ケラマブルー」、サンゴ、ウミガメ。すべて座間味集落から船で向かいます。以下は島のダイビングショップ、ボートシュノーケル・カヤック/SUPの事業者、自然ガイド、そして冬のホエールウォッチング（1〜3月）です。ここは海況がすべて。早めの予約と予備日、そして朝のフェリー欠航予報の確認を——フェリーを止める時化はボートも止めます。

Joy Joy

Zamami village · fun & try dives · snorkel
One of the island's oldest dive shops (since 1983), run by a Zamami-born owner with veteran instructors and deep knowledge of the Kerama dive sites — a well-networked, experienced local choice for fun dives, try dives and snorkel. As guests note, the boat captain can guide in English; confirm your language needs when booking.島でも老舗のダイブショップ（1983年〜）。座間味出身のオーナーとベテランインストラクターが、慶良間のポイントを知り尽くしています。ファンダイブ・体験ダイビング・シュノーケルに対応する、経験豊富で人脈のある地元の一軒。口コミでは船長が英語でガイドできるとのこと。言語面は予約時に確認を。

Zamami village · fun & try dives · snorkel

Coral Divers Club

Zamami village · fun dives · snorkel
A relaxed, lifestyle-oriented shop — late-morning departures, two carefully chosen dives a day, and a clubhouse with evening stargazing. Honestly, the unhurried tempo won't suit divers who want the maximum number of dives, but it's ideal for a calm, unrushed dive stay. The owner doesn't speak English, but his wife can interpret when available, so ask ahead.のんびり暮らすように潜るスタイルの一軒——遅めの出航、厳選2本／日、夜は星空を楽しめるクラブハウスも。正直、本数を多く潜りたい人には向きませんが、ゆったりしたダイブステイには最適。オーナーは英語を話しませんが、奥さまが対応可能なときは通訳できるため、事前に相談を。

Piece

Zamami village · beginners · try dives · boat snorkel
A beginner-focused operator known for its ~90-minute boat snorkel with near-guaranteed turtle sightings and free photo data, plus try dives and licence courses — a good pick for first-timers and day-trippers from Naha. Dive Center NO-Y

Zamami village · dive-and-stay · winter whale watching
A dive shop with its own 9-room lodging (Pension Takatsuki), making dive-and-stay packages easy — the clear water and coral make try dives approachable for non-certified beginners, and in winter it also runs whale-watching boats. Best for travellers who want diving and a bed booked together. English is limited; a translation app helps.9室の宿（ペンション高月）を併設するダイブショップで、泊まって潜るパッケージが手軽。透明度の高い海とサンゴで、未認定の初心者でも体験ダイビングがしやすく、冬はホエールウォッチングの船も出します。ダイビングと宿を一緒に手配したい人向け。英語は多くは通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Zamami Sailing

Zamami village · catamaran snorkel cruise
Snorkel day cruises aboard "Tingara," a stable 11-metre catamaran with a hot shower, a flush toilet, drinks and plenty of shade — very low rocking compared with a small boat. Best for families, older guests and groups who want comfort over a hardcore dive day. English support isn't stated, so confirm when booking.安定感のある全長11mのカタマラン「ティンガーラ」で行くシュノーケルの日中クルーズ。温水シャワー・水洗トイレ・ドリンク・豊富な日陰を備え、小型船に比べて揺れが少ないのが特長。ハードなダイブより快適さを重視する家族・年配・グループ向け。英語対応は明記が無いため予約時に確認を。

Sanyubito — island nature walks

Zamami village · guided nature & viewpoint walks
A guided land option for when you want more than the sea — leisurely nature walks (for example the Chishi viewpoint course, ~2 hours) observing the island's plants, birds and geology, welcoming ages 4 to 80. Best for slow-paced nature lovers and families. Guided viewpoint hikes up Takatsukiyama are also available on the island. English support isn't stated, so confirm when booking.海以外も楽しみたい人向けの陸のガイド——島の植物・野鳥・地形を観察しながらのんびり歩く自然散策（例：稲崎展望台コース、約2時間）。4〜80歳まで参加可。ゆったり自然を楽しみたい人や家族向け。高月山の展望ハイキングのガイドも島内にあります。英語対応は明記が無いため予約時に確認を。

Zamami Whale-Watching Association

Zamami village · humpback whale watching · Jan–Mar
From January to March, humpback whales return to the Kerama waters, and Zamami is one of Japan's best places to see them. The village association (since 1991) coordinates the tours: land spotters on the north-side lookouts radio the boats, giving high encounter rates, under responsible-viewing rules (a 100 m minimum distance). You book directly with one of its affiliated boats — ideally by the day before, as trips are weather-dependent; a couple of boats are marked English-OK. Honest note: it's a coordinating body, not a single booking desk, and in rough seas trips can be cut short or miss whales.1〜3月、ザトウクジラが慶良間の海に戻り、座間味は日本でも屈指の観察スポット。村の協会（1991年〜）がツアーを統括し、北側の展望台の陸上見張りが無線で船を誘導するため遭遇率が高く、100m以上離れる等の責任あるルールのもとで運航します。予約は加盟船の1隻に直接（荒天次第のため前日までが安心）。英語対応を掲げる船も一部あります。正直な補足：単一の予約窓口ではなく統括団体で、時化ではツアーが短縮・不発になることも。
