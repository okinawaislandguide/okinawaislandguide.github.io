Kuroshima · Where to stay

Where to stay on Kuroshima — by travel style黒島の宿 — 旅のスタイル別

Okinawa Island Guide · 2026Okinawa Island Guide · 2026

Kuroshima is a quiet, flat cycling-and-diving island where most people come for the day — so overnight lodging is a small handful of family-run minshuku, mostly around Miyazato village near the port. Nearly all offer free port pickup and free bikes. Book ahead (they're tiny), and bring cash — there are very few shops and no ATM. Staying over buys the island at its calmest, after the day-trippers leave. 黒島は平坦で静かな、サイクリングとダイビングの島。多くは日帰りのため、宿泊は港近くの宮里（みやざと）集落を中心とした数軒の家族経営の民宿だけです。ほぼ全てが港からの無料送迎と無料レンタサイクルに対応。小さいので早めの予約を。商店は非常に少なくATMも無いため現金の用意を。泊まれば、日帰り客が去ったあとの最も静かな島に出会えます。

Minshuku Nakata-so

Miyazato · near Nakamoto Beach · free port pickup
A small minshuku near Nakamoto Beach that guests praise above all for how clean and tidy it is — comfortable enough that solo female travellers settle in easily, with a rooftop for sunbathing and well-kept shared bathrooms. Simple ground-floor rooms with shared facilities. Best for tidy-minded solo travellers and couples wanting a clean, quiet base by the water. English is limited, but a translation app makes things easy.中本海岸近くの小さな民宿。口コミで何より評価されるのは清潔さ・きちんと感で、女性の一人旅でも安心と好評。屋上の日光浴スペース、手入れの行き届いた共用バス。1階のシンプルな客室・共用設備。海の近くで清潔・静かに過ごしたい一人旅やカップル向け。英語は多くは通じませんが、翻訳アプリがあれば大丈夫です。

Minshuku Nodoka

Miyazato · near the port · free bikes
A family-run minshuku loved for its warm host — welcome drinks (awamori included), free bicycles and home-style meals from local ingredients, with review scores among the highest on the island. Rustic in the best way; some rooms have limited air-con, and it's tiny, so book well ahead. Best for travellers who value genuine hospitality over amenities. English is limited; a translation app helps.もてなしの温かさで愛される家族経営の民宿。泡盛付きのウェルカムドリンク、無料レンタサイクル、地元食材の家庭料理で、口コミ評価は島でも上位。良い意味で素朴で、一部客室はエアコン控えめ。小さいので早めの予約を。設備より心のこもったもてなしを求める人向け。英語は多くは通じませんが翻訳アプリが役立ちます。

Minshuku Ah-chan

Miyazato · ~3 min drive from port (free pickup) · free bikes
Known most for its food — meals built on Kuroshima's own vegetables and island seafood (island soups such as aasa seaweed and abasa pufferfish), plus owner-led coconut-crab hunts in season and around a dozen free rental bikes. Simple facilities, and the coconut-crab outing is seasonal. Best for foodies and families wanting a hands-on island experience. English is limited, but a translation app makes it easy.いちばんの魅力は料理。黒島の島野菜や島魚を使った食事（アーサー汁・アバサー汁など）に、季節にはオーナー案内のヤシガニ探しも。無料レンタサイクル約12台。設備はシンプルで、ヤシガニ体験は季節限定。食を楽しみたい人や、体験重視の家族向け。英語は多くは通じませんが翻訳アプリで大丈夫です。

Minshuku Kuroshima

by Nakamoto Beach · dive & snorkel · free port pickup
The most sea-focused choice, right by Nakamoto Beach, running snorkel and dive tours (including toward Panari / Aragusuku Island) with gear rental, plus free bikes and port pickup. Around a dozen rooms and good-value meals; simple minshuku facilities. Best for divers and snorkellers who want the reef on the doorstep. It takes bookings through international sites, so booking in English is workable, though on-site English is basic.いちばん海に近い選択肢。中本海岸のそばで、シュノーケルやダイビングのツアー（パナリ〈新城島〉方面も）や器材レンタル、無料レンタサイクル・港送迎も。客室は約12室、食事はコスパ良好。設備はシンプルな民宿。リーフがすぐ目の前のダイバー・シュノーケラー向け。海外予約サイトに対応し英語での予約は可能ですが、現地の英語は基本的な範囲です。
